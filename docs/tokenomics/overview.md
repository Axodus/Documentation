## Purpose

This document introduces Axodus tokenomics.

## Scope

Tokenomics are under active specification. `$Neurons` is documented as an ecosystem token concept for utility, governance participation, rewards, access, Marketplace use, Academy incentives, and coordination mechanisms.

## Guardrails

Do not describe `$Neurons` as an investment contract, guaranteed appreciating asset, passive income generator, or guaranteed yield token.
