## Purpose

This document defines documentation guardrails for ecosystem rewards.

## Policy Principles

- Rewards should align behavior with ecosystem value.
- Rewards must avoid unsustainable inflation assumptions.
- Reward rules must be transparent.
- Contract-controlled behavior must be verified before final documentation.

## Risk Considerations

Rewards can create farming, sybil, market expectation, and regulatory risk.
