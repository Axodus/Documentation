# Locked Rewards

Status: Draft  
Version: 0.1.0  
Last Updated: 2026-05-16  
Owner: Tokenomics Nucleus

---

## Purpose

Locked rewards are rewards that may be restricted by contract or platform rules.

## Possible Behavior

Locked rewards may be internally usable, non-transferable, restricted to Marketplace use, allowed for voting, or subject to unlock conditions if implemented.

## Contract Enforcement

All restrictions, unlock conditions, and transfer rules must be contract-controlled or platform-enforced before they are documented as active.

## Guardrails

Do not describe locked rewards as freely tradable or guaranteed financial value unless implementation and legal review support that claim.
