## Purpose

Treasury is the institutional capital layer of Axodus.

## Scope

Treasury covers capital allocation, preservation, liquidity, strategy separation, risk limits, governance oversight, and reporting.

## Responsibilities

- Preserve operational runway.
- Allocate capital through approved policies.
- Classify strategy risk.
- Maintain reporting.
- Support accountability.

## Risk Considerations

Treasury activity can lose capital. Treasury documentation must not promise fixed performance.
