## Purpose

This document defines policy principles for Axodus treasury management.

## Objectives

- Capital preservation
- Liquidity management
- Productive allocation
- Transparent reporting
- Governance-controlled execution
- Risk-aware growth

## Strategy Categories

Approved categories may include reserves, conservative yield, productive growth, active strategies, and capped experiments. Prohibited or restricted strategies must be defined before capital deployment.

## Reporting

Treasury actions should define reporting cadence, metrics, owner, and exit criteria.
