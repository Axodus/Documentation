## Purpose

Treasury risk limits define boundaries for capital exposure.

## Limit Categories

- Maximum allocation by strategy
- Maximum allocation by protocol or venue
- Drawdown limits
- Liquidity requirements
- Counterparty limits
- Experimental allocation caps
- Emergency pause conditions

## Governance

Risk limits should be approved and updated through governance-defined processes.
