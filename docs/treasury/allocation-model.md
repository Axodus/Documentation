## Purpose

This document defines how Axodus may allocate capital across products and strategies.

## Allocation Categories

- Reserve
- Product activation
- Growth
- Yield
- Experimentation

## Proposal Requirements

Every allocation proposal should include purpose, amount, strategy, risk classification, expected role, maximum loss tolerance, reporting cadence, exit criteria, and responsible owner.
