## Purpose

Define reporting and accountability records for BBA campaigns, claim reviews, and growth operations.

## Scope

Reports may include campaign report, performance report, claim review record, approval record, content archive, experiment report, launch report, and incident report.

## Report Fields

Campaign reports should include objective, audience, channels, assets, claims reviewed, approvals, metrics, results, limitations, incidents, learnings, and follow-up actions.

## Governance Touchpoints

Governance references should be included when campaigns involve DAO communication, public policy claims, tokenomics, treasury, roadmap commitments, or proposal execution updates.

## Risk Considerations

Reports must distinguish measured outcomes from future projections and must not present campaign results as guaranteed future performance.
