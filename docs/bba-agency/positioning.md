## Purpose

Define how Axodus BBA positions itself as a serious blockchain-native business and advertising agency.

## Scope

This page covers BBA positioning, value proposition, brand personality, tone of voice, audience segments, and identity boundaries.

## Overview

Axodus BBA helps Web3 products, DAOs, creators, and companies communicate clearly, launch responsibly, and grow with measurable, claim-reviewed strategies.

## Model

BBA combines business strategy, Web3 literacy, claim review, ACS-assisted operations, governance awareness, product understanding, transparent reporting, and growth discipline.

## Governance Touchpoints

Governance-sensitive positioning requires review when it references DAO approval, treasury policy, token utility, official status, public launch scope, or community mandates.

## Risk Considerations

BBA must avoid hype, FOMO, fake authority, guaranteed results, token price claims, unsupported partner claims, and vague Web3 buzzwords.
