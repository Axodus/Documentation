## Purpose

Define BBA growth operations as measurable experimentation and funnel improvement without guaranteed outcomes.

## Scope

Growth operations may include acquisition, activation, conversion, retention, onboarding, campaign iteration, analytics planning, and content testing.

## Model

Experiments should define hypothesis, audience, channel, baseline, metric, expected learning, risk level, duration, results, and next action.

## Workflow

Growth work moves through objective setting, funnel mapping, experiment design, claim review, launch, measurement, interpretation, iteration, and reporting.

## Governance Touchpoints

Growth campaigns touching governance participation, token utility, financial products, or treasury outcomes require domain review.

## Risk Considerations

Growth metrics must not be framed as guaranteed sales, guaranteed ROI, guaranteed community growth, or guaranteed product adoption.
