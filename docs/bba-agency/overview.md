## Purpose

Axodus BBA means Blockchain Business & Advertising. It is the specialized agency layer under Axodus Business for blockchain-native positioning, communication, advertising, growth operations, claim review, and launch support.

## Scope

BBA covers positioning, service design, client intake, campaign lifecycle, claim review, growth operations, content and narrative systems, brand architecture, Web3 launch support, DAO and governance communication, Academy and Marketplace alignment, ACS-assisted workflows, reporting, and risk controls.

BBA is not hype marketing, token pump marketing, guaranteed ROI service, fake partnership production, unverified claim publishing, legal certification, or a replacement for Business runtime, Governance, product delivery, or compliance review.

## Ecosystem Role

BBA operates inside [Business](../business/overview.md), supports [Marketplace](../marketplace/overview.md) listings, helps [Academy](../academy/overview.md) tutors and courses communicate responsibly, reviews claims related to [Tokenomics](../tokenomics/overview.md), [Trading](../trading/overview.md), [Treasury](../treasury/overview.md), [DeFi](../defi/overview.md), [Mining](../mining/overview.md), and [Lottery](../lottery/overview.md), and uses [ACS](../acs/overview.md) as advisory support under human validation.

## Risk Considerations

BBA is communication-sensitive. It must not publish unsupported financial, token, trading, treasury, security, audit, partnership, governance, sustainability, or compliance claims. BBA is ROI-aware, but it must never guarantee ROI, sales, growth, token appreciation, user acquisition, or profit.
