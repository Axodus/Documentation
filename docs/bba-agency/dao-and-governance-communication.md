## Purpose

Define how BBA supports governance communication, proposal explainers, DAO updates, and execution narratives.

## Scope

Governance communication may include proposal summaries, voting explainers, roadmap updates, DAO federation communication, plugin launch notes, governance records, and public accountability updates.

## Requirements

Governance content should identify proposal status, decision needed, affected parameters, tradeoffs, risk context, execution constraints, voting window if applicable, and source records.

## Governance Touchpoints

BBA supports communication but does not approve proposals, change vote outcomes, override governance records, or execute governance decisions.

## Risk Considerations

Anti-patterns include implying approval before votes close, hiding material risks, misrepresenting governance authority, or framing votes as legally or technically unconstrained.
