## Purpose

Define BBA support for responsible Web3 launches.

## Scope

Launch support may cover product launches, DApp launches, Marketplace listings, Academy course launches, DAO communication, documentation launches, governance plugin launches, and token or reward program language review.

## Readiness Checklist

Launch materials should confirm product status, target audience, source of truth, claim review, risk disclosures, security status, governance status, support path, analytics, incident path, and reporting plan.

## Workflow

Launch support moves through discovery, readiness check, claim review, asset production, stakeholder approval, launch, monitoring, incident handling, reporting, and archive.

## Governance Touchpoints

Governance review may be required for DAO launches, token or reward programs, treasury-sensitive releases, and public ecosystem commitments.

## Risk Considerations

BBA must not announce products as active, audited, partnered, compliant, or deployed unless records support those claims.
