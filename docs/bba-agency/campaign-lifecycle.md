## Purpose

Define the lifecycle for BBA campaigns from discovery through reporting.

## Scope

Campaigns may support product launches, DAO updates, Marketplace listings, Academy courses, DeFi or Trading onboarding, governance explainers, and ecosystem announcements.

## Workflow

Campaign stages include discovery, strategy, claim review, production, approval, launch, monitoring, optimization, reporting, and archive.

## Campaign Object

Campaign records should include campaign ID, objective, audience, assets, channels, claim review status, risk level, launch status, metrics, approvals, report reference, and archive location.

## Governance Touchpoints

Governance review may be required for DAO campaigns, treasury claims, public roadmap claims, tokenomics claims, and major ecosystem launch communications.

## Risk Considerations

Campaign metrics are measurement tools, not guaranteed outcomes. Campaigns must avoid guaranteed growth, guaranteed acquisition, guaranteed revenue, or token price claims.
