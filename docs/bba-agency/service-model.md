Status: Draft
Version: 0.1.0
Last Updated: 2026-05-17
Owner: Axodus BBA

---

## Purpose

Define BBA services as scoped, measurable, claim-reviewed business workflows.

## Scope

Services may include positioning strategy, campaign strategy, content operations, Web3 claim review, launch support, growth operations, governance communication, and brand direction.

## Service Object

Service records should define service ID, service group, objective, audience, deliverables, required inputs, claim review requirement, governance review requirement, risk level, ACS support, acceptance criteria, reporting requirement, and status.

## Workflow

Requests should enter through Business intake, be scoped into deliverables, receive claim review where needed, run through campaign or content execution, and close with acceptance and reporting.

## Governance Touchpoints

Governance review is required when services affect public governance claims, DAO communication, tokenomics, treasury policy, or ecosystem-wide launch messaging.

## Risk Considerations

BBA services must not promise guaranteed sales, ROI, token growth, community growth, or investment outcomes.
