## Purpose

Define how BBA creates clear, accurate, educational, and risk-aware narratives for Axodus and client-facing materials.

## Scope

Content may include landing pages, launch posts, governance explainers, marketplace listings, Academy course positioning, community updates, email sequences, documentation-friendly copy, and campaign materials.

## Narrative Layers

Narrative should separate ecosystem story, product purpose, user workflow, technical constraints, risk disclosures, status labels, and call to action.

## Review Checklist

Content should be accurate, status-aware, evidence-based, audience-specific, risk-aware, free of hype, and aligned with product, governance, treasury, tokenomics, and compliance constraints.

## Governance Touchpoints

Public governance, treasury, tokenomics, or roadmap narratives require source-of-truth review.

## Risk Considerations

Narrative must not hide risks, imply live integrations without proof, or present planned products as active.
