Last Updated: 2026-05-17

---

## Purpose

Define how BBA supports Academy and Marketplace communication.

## Scope

BBA may support course positioning, tutor positioning, learning path copy, Marketplace listing copy, launch assets, educational product narratives, and reward language review.

## Model

Academy materials must avoid guaranteed career, income, certification value, or reward claims. Marketplace materials must clarify seller status, deliverables, refund policy, token or credit rules, and risk disclosures.

## Governance Touchpoints

Marketplace categories, locked reward utility, token payment claims, official seller status, and certification claims may require Governance, Tokenomics, Academy, or Marketplace review.

## Risk Considerations

Locked rewards must not be described as withdrawable, transferable, sellable, or spendable unless contracts and policy confirm that behavior.
