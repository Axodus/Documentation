## Core Terms

- **Axodus**: Modular DeFi ecosystem centered on governance, treasury, education, business services, trading, token utility, and accountability.
- **Nucleus**: Major functional domain inside Axodus with specific responsibilities, workflows, documentation, risks, and governance touchpoints.
- **Governance**: Coordination heart of Axodus, responsible for proposals, decisions, approvals, execution visibility, constitutional alignment, and DAO federation.
- **Executive DAO**: Strategic and executive coordination layer for roadmap alignment, technical prioritization, and ecosystem execution.
- **Boardroom Council**: Supervisory governance layer for risk, compliance, treasury matters, constitutional alignment, and sensitive decisions.
- **Community DAO**: Open community participation layer for public proposals, voting, signaling, and legitimacy.
- **Constitutional Governance**: Governance model based on shared constitutional principles, constraints, and ecosystem-wide guardrails.
- **Local Governance**: Governance within a specific DAO, sub-DAO, product community, or partner community, with autonomy inside constitutional constraints.
- **DAO Federation**: Model where multiple DAOs operate under shared constitutional principles while preserving local autonomy.
- **Business**: Structured request intake, service coordination, client lifecycle, and delivery runtime.
- **BBA**: Blockchain Business & Advertising, focused on blockchain-native communication, campaign design, growth strategy, and ROI-aware positioning.
- **ACS**: AI Coordination System for analysis, classification, governance assistance, operational review, and decision support.
- **Morpheus**: ACS agent archetype focused on strategy, narrative coherence, governance alignment, and ecosystem sensemaking.
- **Trinity**: ACS agent archetype focused on trading intelligence, operations, execution support, market analysis, P&L review, and risk monitoring.
- **Agent Smith**: ACS agent archetype focused on adversarial review, stress testing, risk challenge, abuse resistance, and controlled friction.
- **Academy**: Education and onboarding nucleus for courses, tutors, Proof of Knowledge, Learn-to-Win, certification, and token utility.
- **Learn-to-Win**: Gamified learning model where users may receive rewards for education-related progress, subject to contract or policy rules.
- **Proof of Knowledge**: Validation model for learning, assessments, certification, or knowledge-based eligibility.
- **Marketplace**: Commercialization layer for products, courses, services, and internal ecosystem utility consumption.
- **Treasury**: Ecosystem capital management layer responsible for allocation, liquidity, exposure, reporting, and risk constraints.
- **Risk Management**: Discipline that identifies, limits, monitors, and reports risks across DeFi, treasury, trading, governance, products, and operations.
- **Accountability**: Transparency layer for reports, roadmap updates, treasury summaries, release notes, governance records, and execution history.
- **DaaS**: DeFi-as-a-Service, meaning modular DeFi infrastructure or services provided through Axodus products, governance, and business workflows.
- **ETF DaaS**: ETF-oriented DeFi-as-a-Service model that may involve basket-like treasury, allocation, or product structures. It must not imply regulated ETF status unless legally confirmed.
- **$Neurons**: Ecosystem token associated with utility, rewards, access, governance participation, and coordination mechanisms.
- **Token Utility**: Practical token use such as access, rewards, governance participation, marketplace use, subscriptions, or internal consumption.
- **Locked Rewards**: Rewards that may be restricted from transfer, sale, withdrawal, or swapping and may be internally usable if contract rules support it.
- **Governance Plugin**: Modular extension that adds governance functionality such as voting, treasury, reporting, or DAO-specific workflows.
- **Proposal Lifecycle**: Path from idea or request to validation, voting, execution, and accountability.
- **Business Runtime**: Lifecycle for intake, classification, analysis, approval, execution, milestone validation, change requests, delivery, and archive.
- **Change Request**: Request to alter approved scope, budget, timeline, technical requirements, or governance impact.
- **Smart Contract**: On-chain program enforcing logic such as tokens, governance, rewards, access, execution, or financial rules.
- **API Key Security**: Protection discipline for API keys, especially in trading products. Withdrawal permissions should be disabled for trading keys.
- **Governance Traceability**: Ability to connect proposals, decisions, votes, executions, and reports across on-chain and off-chain records.
- **Reason Code**: Structured label or explanation attached to decisions, guards, execution results, proposal status, or governance constraints.
- **Canonical Documentation**: Official documentation files in root or `/docs`; excludes old drafts, unreviewed planning notes, and archived materials.
- **Knowledge Pack**: Dense semantic memory file used by agents and contributors to generate consistent documentation and reasoning.
