## Purpose

This page provides the VitePress glossary index. The repository root also contains a canonical `GLOSSARY.md` for readers browsing outside the site.

## Core Terms

- **Axodus**: Modular DeFi ecosystem.
- **Nucleus**: Product or operational domain.
- **Governance**: Coordination and decision-making system.
- **Executive DAO**: Strategic and executive coordination layer.
- **Boardroom Council**: Supervisory and validation layer.
- **Community DAO**: Open participation layer.
- **Constitutional Governance**: Shared principles and operating constraints.
- **Local Governance**: DAO-specific governance within constitutional limits.
- **DAO Federation**: Network of aligned autonomous DAOs.
- **Business**: Request intake and delivery runtime.
- **ACS**: AI coordination and automation support layer.
- **Morpheus**: Strategy and governance alignment agent archetype.
- **Trinity**: Trading and operations agent archetype.
- **Agent Smith**: Adversarial review and risk challenge agent archetype.
- **Academy**: Education and token utility nucleus.
- **Learn-to-Win**: Gamified learning model.
- **Proof of Knowledge**: Learning validation model.
- **Marketplace**: Product and service distribution layer.
- **Treasury**: Capital allocation and preservation layer.
- **Accountability**: Reporting and record publication layer.
- **DaaS**: DeFi-as-a-Service.
- **ETF DaaS**: ETF-oriented DaaS design direction.
- **$Neurons**: Ecosystem utility, governance, reward, access, and coordination token concept.
- **Locked Rewards**: Restricted rewards subject to contract or platform rules.
- **Token Utility**: Functional token use.
- **Trading Strategy**: Risk-bearing market execution tool.
- **Risk Management**: Risk identification, limits, monitoring, and reporting.
