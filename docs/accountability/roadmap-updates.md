## Purpose

Roadmap updates communicate progress, changes, delays, and priorities.

## Required Content

- Current phase
- Completed work
- Changed assumptions
- Blockers
- Next priorities
- Risks and dependencies

## Guardrails

Roadmap updates should not promise fixed delivery dates unless dates are confirmed.
