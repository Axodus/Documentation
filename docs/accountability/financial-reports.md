## Purpose

Financial reports provide transparent context for treasury, revenue, cost, and exposure.

## Report Contents

- Treasury snapshots
- Revenue sources
- Costs
- Strategy exposure
- Risk notes
- Known limitations
- Reporting period

## Guardrails

Reports must avoid misleading certainty and clearly separate realized results from projections.
