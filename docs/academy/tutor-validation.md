---

## Purpose

Tutor validation protects Academy quality and ecosystem trust.

## Scope

Validation may review tutor identity, expertise, course quality, constitutional alignment, reputation, abuse prevention, and governance escalation needs.

## Escalation

Courses or tutors that affect governance, token rewards, financial education, or product access may require additional review.

## Risk Considerations

Poor course quality, misleading financial education, plagiarism, reward farming, and conflicts of interest must be addressed.
