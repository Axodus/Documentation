## Purpose

Academy may integrate with Marketplace to distribute courses, certifications, learning products, and access credentials.

## Scope

Marketplace integration may support course purchases, tutor revenue, commissions, royalties, learning credentials, and token utility.

## Risk Considerations

Commercial listings must not mislead users about rewards, financial outcomes, token value, or product access guarantees.
