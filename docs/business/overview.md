## Purpose

Business is the structured intake and delivery layer of Axodus.

## Scope

Business receives and coordinates client requests, DAO requests, plugin development, integrations, BBA work, consulting, governance implementation, automation, and technical delivery.

## Responsibilities

- Capture requests.
- Classify business and technical impact.
- Define scope, deliverables, budget, approvals, and dependencies.
- Coordinate ACS-assisted analysis.
- Manage execution, milestones, changes, acceptance, reporting, and archive.

## Governance Touchpoints

Business identifies whether a request needs governance approval, treasury review, plugin approval, security review, or constitutional validation.

## Risk Considerations

Business must avoid automatic acceptance of requests, vague scope, undocumented scope expansion, hidden costs, and unapproved governance impact.
