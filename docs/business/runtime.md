## Purpose

This document defines the full Business request lifecycle.

## Lifecycle

1. Intake
2. Classification
3. ACS-assisted analysis
4. Human review
5. Governance requirement check
6. Scope definition
7. Cost and resource estimate
8. Approval
9. Execution
10. Milestone validation
11. Change request handling
12. Delivery
13. Acceptance
14. Reporting
15. Archive

## Change Request Logic

New requests after approval must be logged. Scope expansion requires review. Additional cost or time must be documented. Governance may be required depending on impact. Clients, DAOs, or partners must receive transparent updates.

## Risk Considerations

Silent scope expansion, undocumented approvals, unclear ownership, and missing acceptance criteria create operational and governance risk.
