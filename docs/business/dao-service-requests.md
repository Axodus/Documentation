## Purpose

DAOs and sub-DAOs may request specific tools, integrations, and services through Business.

## Supported Request Types

- Custom governance plugins
- Custom automation
- Custom dashboards
- Product access
- Treasury and reporting tooling
- Community workflows
- Governance implementation support

## Workflow

Requests should be formally captured, scoped, reviewed by ACS where useful, assessed for risk, and approved by governance when they affect shared infrastructure, treasury, security, or constitutional standing.
