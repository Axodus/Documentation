## Purpose

Request intake standardizes how Axodus receives client, DAO, partner, and internal work.

## Required Intake Data

- Requester identity
- Organization, DAO, or partner
- Request type
- Business objective
- Technical scope
- Governance impact
- Risk classification
- Expected deliverables
- Budget or funding model
- Required approvals
- Timeline constraints
- Dependencies

## Review

Business should validate whether the request is feasible, aligned with Axodus principles, and subject to governance, treasury, security, or ACS review.

## Output

The output of intake is a classified request record with scope, next steps, owner, and known uncertainties.
