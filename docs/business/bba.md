## Purpose

BBA means Blockchain Business & Advertising.

## Scope

BBA is a specialized business and advertising layer focused on blockchain-native positioning, campaign strategy, growth operations, tokenized business communication, AI-assisted advertising workflows, and ROI-aware campaign design.

## Responsibilities

- Analyze product positioning.
- Design blockchain-native campaigns.
- Coordinate growth operations.
- Support tokenized business communication.
- Use ACS where useful for research, review, and workflow automation.

## Guardrails

BBA must not become generic marketing hype. Campaigns should be evidence-aware, risk-aware, and aligned with Axodus governance and disclosure standards.
