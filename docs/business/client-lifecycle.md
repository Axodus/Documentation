## Purpose

This document defines a transparent lifecycle for clients, partners, and DAOs that request Axodus services.

## Lifecycle

1. Initial contact
2. Intake
3. Alignment review
4. Scope and risk classification
5. Proposal
6. Approval and funding
7. Delivery milestones
8. Acceptance
9. Reporting
10. Support or archive

## Governance Touchpoints

Requests involving treasury, governance plugins, constitutional standing, product access, or infrastructure changes may require governance approval.
