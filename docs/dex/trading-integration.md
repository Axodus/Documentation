## Purpose

DEX can support Trading through price data, liquidity routes, arbitrage research, execution infrastructure, and market monitoring.

## Use Cases

Use cases include DEX price data, arbitrage research, route analysis, liquidity monitoring, bot execution if implemented and reviewed, and treasury strategy support if approved.

## Review Requirements

Review is required for DEX bot execution, treasury DEX strategy, arbitrage using treasury capital, user-facing DEX trading tools, public performance claims, and MEV protection claims.

## Risk Considerations

Risks include MEV, slippage, gas cost, insufficient liquidity, smart contract vulnerability, and false arbitrage where an apparent opportunity disappears after fees, slippage, or execution.
