## Purpose

DEX fees may compensate LPs, fund protocol operations, support Treasury, or incentivize liquidity if implemented and approved.

## Fee Types

Possible fee types include swap fees, LP fees, protocol fees, treasury fees if approved, router or aggregation fees if implemented, listing service fees if Business policy defines them, and liquidity incentive budgets if approved.

Fee mechanics must not be described as guaranteed revenue, guaranteed liquidity, or token price support.

## Fee Record

Fee records should include fee type, scope, rate or formula, asset, amount, destination, pool or route, period, governance reference, contract reference, status, and reporting reference.

## Governance Requirements

Governance is required for changing swap fees, protocol fees, treasury fees, router fees, fee destinations, liquidity incentives, fees for NEURONS-related pools, or public fee claims.

## Reporting

Reports should include period, volume, gross fees, LP fees, protocol fees, treasury fees if any, incentive costs, net protocol revenue if applicable, pool breakdown if safe, and limitations.

## Risk Considerations

Risks include fee opacity, LP revenue overpromise, treasury revenue overpromise, incentive cost, and contract mismatch.
