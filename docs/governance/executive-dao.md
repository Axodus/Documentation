## Purpose

The Executive DAO is the strategic and executive coordination layer in the current Axodus governance design.

## Scope

It covers roadmap alignment, core development coordination, technical prioritization, and operational oversight.

## Responsibilities

- Align core development with governance priorities.
- Validate roadmap direction.
- Coordinate high-impact implementation work.
- Prepare proposals or execution plans for governance review.
- Support operational continuity.

## Governance Touchpoints

The Executive DAO may interact with the Boardroom Council and Community DAO. Final representation, voting rights, and eligibility rules are not yet defined in this documentation.

## Risk Considerations

The Executive DAO must avoid centralizing unchecked authority. Its decisions should remain auditable and bounded by constitutional, security, and accountability requirements.
