## Purpose

This document explains how DAOs may request custom governance plugins or extensions.

## Scope

It covers DAO-specific voting plugins, governance extensions, dashboards, reporting tools, automation, and approval flows.

## Request Flow

1. DAO submits request through Business.
2. Business captures requester identity, objective, scope, governance impact, budget, timeline, and dependencies.
3. ACS may assist with analysis and risk classification.
4. Human review validates feasibility.
5. Governance approves relevant development or deployment steps.
6. Security reviews sensitive contracts, permissions, and execution paths.
7. Delivery is documented and archived.

## Example

A DAO may request a custom voting plugin for its local community. The request must be scoped, reviewed, and approved before being treated as an official Axodus-supported component.
