## Purpose

This document defines risk categories for trading products.

## Risk Categories

- Market risk
- Liquidity risk
- Exchange risk
- Strategy risk
- API key risk
- Smart contract risk for on-chain strategies
- Operational risk
- Governance risk
- Leverage and liquidation risk

## Required Controls

Trading products should define capital limits, loss limits, strategy status, supported exchanges, permission requirements, monitoring, and shutdown conditions.
