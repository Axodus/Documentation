## Purpose

Trading is both internal financial infrastructure and a user-facing strategy product line.

## Scope

Trading may support ecosystem treasury strategies, market making, arbitrage, trend following, risk-managed automation, user-side trading bots, strategy subscriptions, and access rules.

## Responsibilities

- Validate strategies internally before public access.
- Document risk, assumptions, and limits.
- Provide reporting for treasury or user-facing products.
- Maintain API key and exchange permission guidance.

## Risk Considerations

Trading can lose money. No strategy, bot, or automation module should be described as guaranteed profit.
