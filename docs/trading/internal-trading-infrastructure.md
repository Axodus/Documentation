## Purpose

Internal trading infrastructure supports controlled ecosystem strategy validation and treasury-related operations.

## Scope

It may include treasury strategies, documented execution, risk-managed automation, performance reporting, and governance oversight.

## Requirements

- Strategy objective and market conditions.
- Capital limits and loss limits.
- Exchange or protocol assumptions.
- Operational owner.
- Reporting cadence.
- Governance approval when treasury capital is involved.

## Risk Notice

Internal trading does not guarantee returns and may produce losses.
