## Purpose

This document describes user-facing strategy access concepts.

## Scope

Users may access selected trading strategies based on `$Neurons` balance, subscription, milestone, certification, NFT license, or governance-defined rules.

## User Control

Strategies may run on user-owned CEX accounts or wallets. Users provide their own API keys and retain responsibility for capital exposure, exchange permissions, risk settings, and account security.

## Example

A low-cost or free strategy may be offered to users who hold a minimum amount of `$Neurons`, allowing them to operate a limited strategy such as a futures scalper on their own Binance, KuCoin, or compatible account. This is a tool, not a profit guarantee.

## Risk Notice

Users can lose funds. Axodus must provide risk warnings and permission guidance before activation.
