## Purpose

Mining can consume significant energy and create heat, noise, hardware waste, and local operational impacts. Sustainability documentation must be evidence-based.

## Responsible Operations Themes

Themes include energy transparency, hardware efficiency, cooling efficiency, heat and noise considerations, hardware lifecycle, verified renewable or offset claims, local compliance, and responsible user marketing.

## Sustainability Record Fields

Fields may include operation ID, reporting period, energy usage estimate or actual, energy source if known, renewable claim status, cooling method, efficiency metrics, heat or noise notes, hardware lifecycle notes, evidence reference, and limitations.

## Prohibited Claims

Do not claim carbon neutrality, renewable-powered operation, zero impact, guaranteed green mining, environmental risk-free status, sustainability without defined basis, or offset claims without evidence.

## Safe Language

Use language such as energy profile, efficiency-focused, subject to energy source verification, sustainability review pending, responsible operations, energy usage estimate, evidence-based sustainability claim, and active if verified.
