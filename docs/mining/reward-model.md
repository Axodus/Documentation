## Purpose

This document defines cautious reward language for Mining.

## Reward Inputs

Mining rewards may depend on network conditions, energy costs, hardware uptime, pool terms, asset price, fees, and operational expenses.

## Guardrails

Do not promise fixed mining returns. Any reward estimate must identify assumptions and risk.
