## Purpose

This document defines planning assumptions for Mining products.

## Product Elements

Mining products may include hashrate acquisition, hosted miners, pooled mining, managed operations, reporting dashboards, or DAO-controlled mining allocations.

## Governance Touchpoints

Capital allocation, provider selection, custody model, and revenue distribution should require governance review when ecosystem treasury or DAO funds are involved.
