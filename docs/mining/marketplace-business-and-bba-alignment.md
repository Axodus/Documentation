## Purpose

Mining can be commercialized through Marketplace and Business, but mining-related services and products are claim-sensitive.

## Marketplace Listings

Possible listings include mining education courses, mining dashboard access, mining subscriptions if approved, hardware setup services, mining report templates, validator node services, and mining infrastructure consulting.

## Business Interfaces

Business interfaces include client intake, feasibility study, infrastructure planning, monitoring service, custom quote, and change request handling. Mining services often require custom scope.

## BBA Interfaces

BBA supports ROI, reward, profit, sustainability, and performance claim review; product positioning; launch campaigns; listing copy; and sustainability language review.

## Claim Review Triggers

Triggers include ROI or profitability claims, daily reward claims, payback period claims, passive income claims, sustainability claims, hardware performance claims, pool or validator performance claims, user payout claims, treasury revenue claims, and legal or tax claims.

## Risk Considerations

Risks include overpromised profit, service scope confusion, seller quality issues, sustainability overclaim, and marketplace liability.
