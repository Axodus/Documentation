## Purpose

Mining decisions can affect treasury capital, operating costs, user expectations, payout liabilities, token utility, sustainability claims, and public trust.

## Governance-Sensitive Items

Sensitive items include mining product activation, treasury allocation, payout policy, fee policy, tokenomics policy, risk limits, sustainability claims, and emergency actions.

## Routing Levels

- No Governance: educational content or internal research.
- Governance Visibility: mining report or infrastructure status update.
- Governance Review: new mining model, payout policy draft, or marketplace mining category.
- Formal Proposal: treasury allocation, public user product, token payment policy, fee policy, or large infrastructure commitment.
- Emergency Review: security breach, payout error, major unprofitability event, compliance pause, hardware incident, or facility incident.

## Reason Codes

Reason codes include `MINING_GOVERNANCE_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, `TREASURY_MINING_ALLOCATION_REQUIRED`, `MINING_PAYOUT_POLICY_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, `TOKENOMICS_MINING_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, `MINING_CLAIM_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, `MINING_COMPLIANCE_GATE_REQUIRED`, and `EMERGENCY_MINING_ACTION`.
