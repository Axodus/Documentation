## Purpose

Mining reporting must distinguish rewards from profit and include assumptions, costs, incidents, limitations, and governance references.

## Report Types

Reports may include mining operations, reward, payout, treasury mining, profitability estimate, uptime, validator, sustainability, incident, and governance records.

## Mining Report Fields

Fields should include report ID, period, operations, network or asset, hashrate or validator status, uptime, gross rewards, fees, energy costs, hosting costs, maintenance costs, net estimate, payouts, held rewards, retained rewards, user liabilities, incidents, governance references, assumptions, limitations, and no-guarantee note.

## Accountability Records

Records include operation, hardware, pool, reward, payout, treasury, and incident records.

## Quality Rules

Reports must distinguish gross rewards from net estimates, disclose costs or exclusions, state assumptions and period, include negative periods or downtime, disclose missing data, avoid presenting historical results as future guarantees, protect sensitive data, and reference governance for material actions.
