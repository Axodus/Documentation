## Purpose

ACS supports DeFi research, strategy analysis, protocol review, risk summaries, claim review, reporting, and governance briefs.

## Agent Roles

Trinity reviews strategy performance, market risk, liquidity risk, treasury exposure, and yield or loss reporting.

Agent Smith performs adversarial review, detects yield overclaims, identifies attack surfaces, challenges protocol assumptions, and flags governance or treasury bypass.

Morpheus aligns DeFi with ecosystem strategy, governance narrative, documentation coherence, and DaaS positioning.

## Workflow

1. DeFi request, product, protocol, or strategy is received.
2. Context is loaded from DeFi, Treasury, Governance, Tokenomics, and Security docs.
3. Trinity reviews strategy, market, or treasury context.
4. Agent Smith reviews risks and claims.
5. Morpheus reviews positioning and governance alignment.
6. Human or domain reviewer validates.
7. Governance, security, or treasury review is routed when required.
8. Output is used for documentation, scope, report, or proposal.
9. Accountability record is created.

## Output Types

Outputs may include protocol summaries, strategy reviews, risk reports, claim review notes, and governance briefs.

## Boundaries

ACS can analyze, summarize, flag risks, draft reports, prepare handoffs, and suggest safer language. ACS cannot approve DeFi strategies, approve treasury allocation, deploy contracts without authority, move funds, guarantee yield, publish sensitive claims without review, or replace governance and security review.
