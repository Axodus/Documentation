## Purpose

This document defines cautious principles for derivative-related documentation.

## Scope

Derivatives can create leverage, liquidation, counterparty, oracle, and complex strategy risk.

## Governance

Derivative strategies should require risk classification, capital limits, reporting, and governance approval before treasury use.
