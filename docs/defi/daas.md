## Purpose

DaaS means DeFi as a Service: the Axodus model for packaging decentralized finance capabilities into structured, governable, risk-aware, service-oriented products.

## Scope

DaaS may serve internal Axodus teams, DAOs, businesses, partners, and users. It does not mean guaranteed yield, risk-free income, unreviewed protocol access, or unmanaged investment activity.

## Service Categories

- DeFi strategy packaging.
- DAO DeFi services.
- Treasury DeFi services.
- Vault or pool products if implemented.
- Liquidity services when approved.
- Staking services when reviewed.
- Reporting services.
- Academy-based education services.

## Request Object

A DaaS request should record requester, requester type, objective, target assets, target protocols, user-facing or treasury-facing scope, risk tolerance, expected service, custody model, governance requirement, treasury impact, tokenomics impact, security review requirement, reporting requirement, and status.

## Lifecycle

1. Request intake.
2. Objective and scope definition.
3. Risk classification.
4. Protocol research.
5. Treasury or user impact review.
6. Governance review if material.
7. Security review if contracts or integrations are involved.
8. Service design.
9. Implementation or configuration.
10. Monitoring and reporting.
11. Iteration or deprecation.

## Risk Considerations

DaaS risk includes service overpromise, external protocol dependency, custody confusion, hidden treasury exposure, and regulatory uncertainty.
