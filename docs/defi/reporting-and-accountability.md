## Purpose

DeFi products and strategies require reporting that includes exposure, performance, fees, risks, incidents, protocol status, treasury impact, user-facing status, and governance decisions.

## Report Types

Reports may include DeFi product reports, strategy reports, vault reports, treasury DeFi reports, protocol review reports, risk reports, incident reports, and governance records.

## Report Fields

Reports should include report identifier, period, product or strategy, status, assets, protocols, exposure, deposits, withdrawals, yield or loss, fees, slippage or impermanent loss where relevant, liquidity status, risk limit status, incidents, governance references, security review status, limitations, and a no-yield-guarantee note.

## Accountability Records

Records may include strategy approval records, protocol whitelist records, execution receipts, incident records, and disclosure records.

## Quality Rules

Do not cherry-pick positive yield while hiding losses, fees, lockups, failed assumptions, or risk. Reports should distinguish gross from net values, identify assumptions, and link to governance or security references when relevant.
