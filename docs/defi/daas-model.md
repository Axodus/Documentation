## Purpose

DeFi-as-a-Service packages DeFi infrastructure, access, strategies, or tooling into governed services.

## Scope

DaaS may support DAOs, partners, users, and internal treasury operations. Capital-sensitive services should require governance, risk, and reporting controls.

## Guardrails

DaaS must not be described as risk-free or guaranteed yield.
