## Purpose

Lottery parameters affect fairness, treasury, compliance, user safety, and trust. Governance must control material rules and public activation decisions.

## Scope

Governance-sensitive items include game modes, draw parameters, prize policy, fee policy, tokenomics settings, compliance gates, security configuration, and emergency actions.

## Routing Levels

- No Governance: internal concept notes and non-public simulations.
- Governance Visibility: draft game model or research update.
- Governance Review: testnet draw, public lottery documentation, or parameter change proposal.
- Formal Proposal: real draw activation, game model approval, fee or prize policy, token payment, or treasury reserve policy.
- Emergency Review: randomness failure, contract vulnerability, prize accounting error, or compliance pause.

## Reason Codes

Reason codes include `LOTTERY_GOVERNANCE_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, `GAME_MODEL_APPROVAL_REQUIRED`, `PRIZE_POLICY_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, `LOTTERY_COMPLIANCE_GATE_REQUIRED`, `RANDOMNESS_SECURITY_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, `TREASURY_PRIZE_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, and `EMERGENCY_DRAW_ACTION`.

## Governance Touchpoints

Governance should approve material game rules, draw schedules, fee policies, prize allocation, emergency pause authority, product activation, and public launch decisions.
