## Purpose

Marketplace activity must be reportable so Governance, Treasury, Business, Academy, sellers, and operators can understand listings, orders, revenue, settlement, refunds, credits, disputes, quality, and risks.

## Report Types

Reports may include marketplace activity, listing, seller, fee, settlement, credits, dispute, Academy marketplace, Business / BBA, and incident reports.

## Marketplace Report Fields

Fields may include report ID, reporting period, active listings, new listings, orders, gross volume, net revenue, fees, seller payouts, refunds, credits issued, credits spent, credits expired, open liabilities, disputes, seller status changes, sensitive listing reviews, governance references, incidents, and limitations.

## Accountability Records

Records include listing records, seller records, order records, payment records, settlement records, credit records, dispute records, and governance records.

## Quality Rules

Reports must distinguish gross volume from revenue, separate seller payouts from treasury fees, disclose refunds and disputes, report locked credit liabilities, label estimates, protect sensitive data, and avoid presenting marketplace results as guaranteed future revenue.
