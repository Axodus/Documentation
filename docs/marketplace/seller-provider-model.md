# Seller / Provider Model

---

## Purpose

Marketplace trust depends on clear seller, tutor, creator, and provider status.

## Seller Types

Seller types may include Axodus Official, Academy Tutor, Business Provider, BBA Provider, DAO Provider, Partner, Community Creator, and External Client Provider. Official or partner status must only be shown when approved.

## Seller Profile Fields

Profile fields may include seller ID, display name, seller type, profile description, verification status, quality status, specialties, reviewed credentials or references, linked listings, payout preferences, support channel, dispute history summary if policy allows, policy acceptance, and status.

## Verification Statuses

Verification statuses may include Unverified, Basic Verified, Qualified, Official, Suspended, Banned, and Deprecated.

## Quality Statuses

Quality statuses may include New, Active, Trusted, Needs Review, Probation, and Suspended.

## Responsibilities

Sellers must provide accurate information, deliver what is promised, avoid unsupported claims, follow refund and support policy, respect privacy, disclose requirements, maintain communication, follow category rules, accept dispute processes, and keep credentials current.

## Tutor Requirements

Tutor listings should define course objectives, prerequisites, learning outcomes, certification status, reward rules, support expectations, and quality review status if policy requires it.
