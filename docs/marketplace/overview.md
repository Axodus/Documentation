## Purpose

Axodus Marketplace is the ecosystem commercial exchange layer for products, services, courses, digital assets, templates, subscriptions, DAO services, and internal ecosystem offers.

## Scope

Marketplace covers listings, sellers, buyers, payments, settlement, fees, credits, locked rewards, reputation, disputes, refunds, security, governance, treasury reporting, and ACS-assisted review.

Marketplace is not a guaranteed-income platform, investment marketplace, unreviewed token shop, seller revenue guarantee, buyer outcome guarantee, legal compliance certifier, or replacement for Business, Academy, Governance, Treasury, or security review.

## Ecosystem Role

Marketplace connects [Academy](../academy/overview.md) courses and tutors, [Business](../business/overview.md) service delivery, [BBA Agency](../bba-agency/overview.md) positioning and claim review, [Tokenomics](../tokenomics/overview.md) utility if approved, [Treasury](../treasury/overview.md) settlement reporting, [DEX](../dex/overview.md) and [DeFi](../defi/overview.md) integrations if implemented, [Governance](../governance/overview.md) policy controls, [ACS](../acs/overview.md) advisory support, and [Accountability](../accountability/overview.md) records.

## Risk Considerations

Marketplace activity can create seller claim risk, buyer expectation risk, payment risk, token volatility, locked credit liability, settlement risk, refund disputes, regulated category risk, privacy risk, and fraud risk. Listings must not promise guaranteed revenue, ROI, buyer results, DeFi yield, trading profit, or token value.
