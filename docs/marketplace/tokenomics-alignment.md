## Purpose

Marketplace may become a major utility surface for NEURONS, but tokenomics must be described through utility, access, credits, discounts, and ecosystem participation, not investment returns or price appreciation.

## Interfaces

Interfaces may include NEURONS payments for selected listings if implemented, discounts if policy defines them, locked reward spending only if approved, seller or buyer rewards if defined, product access if approved, governance review for marketplace policies, and fee routing if implemented.

## Policy Questions

Questions include which listings accept NEURONS, whether locked rewards are usable, which sellers accept credits, who settles seller value, who funds discounts, whether rewards are locked or unlocked, whether marketplace actions affect governance power, and whether any burn, liquidity, or treasury fee mechanics are active.

## Record Fields

Tokenomics records should capture order ID, token used, token amount, locked or unlocked status, credit amount, discount amount, reward amount, fee amount, destination, settlement model, governance reference, and report reference.

## Risk Considerations

Risks include investment framing, locked reward liability, reward abuse, seller confusion, contract-policy mismatch, and governance capture.
