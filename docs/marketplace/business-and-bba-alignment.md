## Purpose

Marketplace can commercialize Business and BBA services, but custom work must remain scoped, delivered, and validated through Business runtime.

## Business Interfaces

Business interfaces include service packages, client requests, DAO service requests, milestones, change requests, and acceptance logic.

## BBA Interfaces

BBA supports listing copy, claim review, launch campaigns, seller positioning, service catalog design, and growth operations.

## Service Request Flow

1. Buyer selects or requests a service.
2. Marketplace collects initial context.
3. Business classifies scope.
4. BBA reviews positioning or claims if needed.
5. Quote or fixed scope is confirmed.
6. Order is created.
7. Delivery runtime starts.
8. Milestones, change requests, acceptance, disputes, settlement, and reporting are tracked.

## Claim Review Triggers

Triggers include guaranteed business results, ROI, marketing performance, official partnerships, case studies, Trading or DeFi service claims, security or audit claims, compliance claims, and token or reward claims.
