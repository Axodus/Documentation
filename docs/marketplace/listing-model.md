## Purpose

A listing is the public commercial representation of a product, service, course, subscription, template, digital asset, or access offer.

## Listing Categories

Categories may include Course, Service, Digital Download, Subscription, Product Access, DAO Service, DeFi Service, Trading Service, BBA Service, and MCP or Agent Service. DeFi and Trading listings require specific risk disclosures and claim review.

## Listing Fields

Fields may include listing ID, title, slug, category, seller or provider ID, descriptions, audience, included items, not included items, requirements, delivery model, delivery schedule, price, accepted assets, discounts, credits, locked reward eligibility if approved, refund policy, support policy, claims, evidence, risk disclosures, claim review status, quality status, governance status, and status.

## Status Model

Statuses may include Draft, Pending Review, Needs Revision, Approved, Published, Paused, Sold Out or Unavailable, Deprecated, Rejected, and Suspended.

## Claim Review Triggers

Claim review is required for income, ROI, trading performance, DeFi yield, certification, official partner, audit, security, legal, compliance, guaranteed result, token utility, transferability, or locked reward spending claims.

## Quality Checklist

Listings should have clear titles, correct categories, visible seller status, accurate deliverables, clear exclusions, clear pricing, visible refund policy, supported claims, risk disclosures where needed, validated token or credit rules, and governance review for sensitive categories.
