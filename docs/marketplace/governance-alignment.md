## Purpose

Marketplace policy affects economic activity, seller access, buyer protection, token utility, locked rewards, fees, treasury flows, disputes, and public trust.

## Governance-Sensitive Items

Sensitive items include listing policy, seller policy, fee policy, token policy, locked reward spending, dispute policy, sensitive product categories, seller settlement, refund reserves, and treasury routing.

## Routing Levels

- No Governance: basic copy edit or non-sensitive formatting update.
- Governance Visibility: new category research or marketplace report.
- Governance Review: seller verification rules, locked reward spending proposal, or sensitive category policy.
- Formal Proposal: marketplace fee change, token payment policy, treasury settlement model, dispute policy change, or official marketplace program.
- Emergency Review: fraud incident, payment issue, security breach, seller abuse, or credit liability issue.

## Reason Codes

Reason codes include `MARKETPLACE_GOVERNANCE_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, `LISTING_POLICY_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, `SELLER_POLICY_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, `TOKENOMICS_MARKETPLACE_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, `FEE_POLICY_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, `DISPUTE_POLICY_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, `TREASURY_SETTLEMENT_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, and `SENSITIVE_LISTING_REVIEW_REQUIRED`.
