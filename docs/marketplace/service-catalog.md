## Purpose

The Marketplace service catalog is the structured surface where Axodus, Business, BBA, Academy, DAOs, creators, and providers can offer services.

## Service Groups

Service groups may include Business services, BBA services, Academy services, DAO services, DeFi / DaaS services, Trading services, MCP / Agent services, and creative digital assets.

## Service Listing Fields

Fields should include service ID, service group, title, objective, deliverables, exclusions, prerequisites, delivery model, revision policy, acceptance criteria, price or quote model, accepted assets, required reviews, disclosures, provider status, and support policy.

## Delivery Models

Delivery models may include fixed scope, milestone-based delivery, subscription, consultation, managed service, DAO request, and custom quote. Custom work should route through Business runtime before pricing or delivery commitment.

## Catalog Rules

Services must have clear deliverables, avoid guaranteed business results, route sensitive services through review, use BBA-safe claim language, and include risk disclosures for Trading, DeFi, Treasury, or token-related work.
