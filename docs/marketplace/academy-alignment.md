## Purpose

Marketplace and Academy are tightly connected through courses, tutors, certifications, Proof of Knowledge paths, Learn-to-Win flows, and educational assets.

## Interfaces

Marketplace may commercialize paid courses, tutor services, learning bundles, templates, certifications if approved, and internal reward utility if policy and contracts allow it.

## Course Listing Fields

Course listings should include course ID, title, tutor ID, level, prerequisites, learning outcomes, modules, duration, price, accepted assets, reward policy, locked or unlocked reward status, certification or badge status, refund policy, tutor support policy, quality status, and claim review status.

## Tutor Quality Rules

Tutor profiles must be clear. Course claims must be supported. Certification claims require review. Income or career claims must not be guaranteed. Reward claims must match Academy and Tokenomics policy.

## Reward Language Rules

Free-course rewards should be described as locked or restricted if policy defines them that way. Locked rewards must not be described as withdrawable, transferable, sellable, or spendable unless policy and contracts confirm it. Rewards are not income guarantees.
