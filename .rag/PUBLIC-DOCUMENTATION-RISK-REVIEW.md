# PUBLIC-DOCUMENTATION-RISK-REVIEW

## Primary Risk Statement

The primary public-documentation risk is not lack of content volume. It is
coherence risk across an already large public corpus.

## Risk Register

| Risk ID | Risk | Affected surface | Freeze classification |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| PDR-01 | Mixed metadata discipline across priority pages | `overview`, secondary directory landing pages | `REVIEW_REQUIRED` |
| PDR-02 | Governance concept pages read as active institutions or authority bodies | `docs/governance/` | `CLAIM_REVIEW_REQUIRED` |
| PDR-03 | Tokenomics pages read as settled issuance, supply, reward, or utility mechanics | `docs/tokenomics/` | `BOUNDARY_REVIEW_REQUIRED` |
| PDR-04 | Treasury pages read as evidence of live capital operations or approved thresholds | `docs/treasury/` | `BOUNDARY_REVIEW_REQUIRED` |
| PDR-05 | Trading pages read as active user access, live execution, or performance posture | `docs/trading/` | `CLAIM_REVIEW_REQUIRED` |
| PDR-06 | Mining pages read as active infrastructure, profitability, or payout evidence | `docs/mining/` | `CLAIM_REVIEW_REQUIRED` |
| PDR-07 | Lottery pages read as active public availability, funded prize commitments, or legal readiness | `docs/lottery/` | `BOUNDARY_REVIEW_REQUIRED` |
| PDR-08 | Public pages interpreted as more settled than the private request-state allows | `docs/` versus `requests/` | `REVIEW_REQUIRED` |

## Highest-Risk Domains for REQ-02

1. `docs/tokenomics/`
2. `docs/treasury/`
3. `docs/trading/`
4. `docs/governance/`
5. `docs/mining/`
6. `docs/lottery/`

## Low-Risk Principle for Next Batch

The lowest-risk next move is not creating new pages. The lowest-risk next move
is freezing terminology, status, maturity, claim, and boundary decisions on a
bounded set of already-existing pages.

## Risk Decision

Proceed to REQ-02 in `review and decision only` mode.
