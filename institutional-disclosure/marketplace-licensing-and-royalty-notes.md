## Purpose

This guide captures the private modular-product and recurring-royalty direction
for Marketplace without converting it into a sales offer, legal contract,
public commercial page, or investor narrative.

## Licensing Scope

Marketplace may be treated as a licensable software product that can be
deployed outside the Axodus federated ecosystem. The scope includes the
software boundary, deployment boundary, branding flexibility, and continuing
commercial alignment concept.

Any licensed deployment remains subject to separate governance, business,
treasury, and legal decisions before externalization.

## Modular Product Boundary

The modular-product boundary may include:

- Marketplace storefront software;
- catalog logic;
- transaction interface;
- custom product logic;
- custom catalog logic;
- custom user-experience layers;
- custom branding and UI.

This boundary does not automatically include Axodus central treasury
participation, live tokenomics rights, or public commercialization rights.
