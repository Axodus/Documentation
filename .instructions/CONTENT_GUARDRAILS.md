## No Guaranteed Returns

Never claim guaranteed profit, guaranteed APY, risk-free yield, fixed investment return unless legally and contractually defined, passive income certainty, or treasury performance certainty.

## DeFi Risk Disclosure

Any document discussing DeFi, trading, staking, mining, treasury, derivatives, ETFs, or token economics must include appropriate risk context.

## Token Language

Allowed framing for `$Neurons`:

- utility token;
- governance participation asset;
- ecosystem reward unit;
- access and coordination mechanism;
- incentive alignment layer.

Avoid framing `$Neurons` as an investment contract, guaranteed appreciating asset, passive income generator, or guaranteed yield token.

## Trading Language

Trading products must be described as tools, strategies, or automation modules. Never imply that trading bots guarantee profit. Users retain responsibility for their own accounts, API keys, exchange permissions, risk settings, and capital exposure.

## Governance Language

Governance is a decision-making and coordination system. Governance votes do not override legal, security, or compliance constraints.

## Business Language

Business services are structured intake, analysis, delivery, and validation workflows. Do not imply automatic acceptance of every client request.

## Academy Language

Academy rewards must be documented carefully. Free-course rewards may be locked or internally usable depending on contract rules. Paid-course rewards may have different unlock conditions. Do not claim final token behavior unless implemented and verified.
