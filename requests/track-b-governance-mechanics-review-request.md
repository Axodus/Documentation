## Review Scope

Evaluate clarity of the three governance levels, centralization risk,
premature decentralization risk, agent authority, founder dependency, voting
limits, conflicts between Executive DAO, Boardroom, and Community DAO, legal
and reputational risks, and items that must remain TBD.

and must not resolve deferred governance parameters without authorization.
