## Review Scope

Block language implying return, appreciation, expected price, guaranteed
scarcity, fundraising, investment, yield, guaranteed staking reward, fixed
valuation, market demand, price support, public sale, airdrop promise, public
offering, or financial advice.

Assess whether supply and halving language, PoK Minter, validator incentives,
treasury allocation, and liquidity pairing require additional governance or
legal limits.

## Acceptance

Track C baseline closure.
