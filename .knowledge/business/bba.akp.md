## 2. BBA Thesis

Axodus BBA exists because blockchain products require a different kind of business communication.

In Web3, marketing cannot be detached from governance, tokenomics, product readiness, community trust, treasury transparency, regulatory uncertainty, and technical truth.

BBA must help projects communicate clearly, grow responsibly, position products accurately, and design campaigns that support real business outcomes.

BBA connects Business, ACS, Marketplace, Academy, Governance, and product nuclei.

BBA should be ROI-aware, but it must avoid misleading performance promises.

## 7. BBA Claim Review

claim_categories:
  product_status:
    allowed:
      - live_if_verified
      - beta_if_testing
      - planned_if_not_built
      - experimental_if_limited_scope
    prohibited:
      - claiming_live_when_not_implemented

  token_claim:
    allowed:
      - utility
      - access
      - reward
      - governance_participation
    prohibited:
      - guaranteed_appreciation
      - guaranteed_income
      - passive_income_claim

  financial_claim:
    allowed:
      - risk_disclosed_context
      - historical_metrics_if_verified_and_contextualized
    prohibited:
      - guaranteed_profit
      - risk_free_returns
      - fixed_APY_unless_contractually_and_legally_defined

  audit_claim:
    allowed:
      - audited_by_named_party_if_verified
    prohibited:
      - audited_without_actual_audit

  partnership_claim:
    allowed:
      - partnership_if_confirmed
    prohibited:
      - implying_partnership_from_informal_conversation
