## 1. Business Identity

business_role:
  primary:
    - structured_intake_runtime
    - service_coordination_layer
    - client_and_DAO_gateway
    - delivery_orchestration_layer
    - scope_control_system
    - governance_sensitive_request_router
    - ACS_consumption_layer
    - business_accountability_layer

business_is_not:
  - informal_sales_channel_only
  - generic_agency_without_governance
  - unrestricted_client_request_acceptor
  - replacement_for_governance
  - replacement_for_security_review
  - replacement_for_legal_review
  - automatic_delivery_promise_engine
  - hype_marketing_department

business_thesis:
  - Axodus Business converts requests into structured, scoped, reviewable, governable, and deliverable work.
  - Business is the entry point for many external and internal requests.
  - Business must classify requests, identify responsible nuclei, define scope, estimate effort, map risks, detect governance touchpoints, coordinate delivery, manage change requests, and create acceptance records.
  - Business must protect Axodus from vague commitments, scope creep, unreviewed governance impacts, unverified financial claims, and uncontrolled delivery expectations.
  - Business is expected to be one of the largest consumers of ACS because ACS helps analyze requests, classify risks, detect missing information, draft scopes, and prepare handoffs.

    - reviews_governance_sensitive_requests
    - validates_DAO_plugin_requests
    - approves_policy_sensitive_changes
    - may_require_accountability_records

  ACS:
    - analyzes_requests
    - classifies_risk
    - drafts_scopes
    - detects_missing_information
    - supports_change_request_analysis

  Academy:
    - receives_training_course_and_partner_program_requests
    - coordinates_tutor_or_partner_programs
    - may require marketplace and reward policy review

  Marketplace:
    - supports service/product listing and commerce flows
    - handles course and educational product commercialization
    - may require fee and payment policy

  Trading:
    - receives strategy access or setup requests
    - must include risk and API security review
    - no profit guarantees

  Treasury:
    - may fund or receive revenue from business activities
    - treasury-sensitive requests require governance and reporting

  Tokenomics:
    - reward or token utility requests must align with policy and contracts
    - no investment language

  Accountability:
    - records material decisions, delivery outcomes, and reporting

## 12. Public Documentation Guidance

When creating public Business docs:

- Explain Business as a structured runtime.
- Define intake, classification, scoping, delivery, change requests, and acceptance.
- Explain DAO service requests and plugin requests.
- Explain BBA as specialized blockchain business and advertising.
- Explain ACS support and governance boundaries.
- Include risk, compliance, and accountability notes.
- Avoid sales hype.
- Avoid unverified claims.
