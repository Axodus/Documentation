## Purpose

This folder contains the deep semantic knowledge base for the Business nucleus of Axodus.

These files are not public-facing documentation pages. They are agent-oriented knowledge packs designed to help coding agents, documentation agents, ACS agents, governance agents, business operators, and future MCP systems generate accurate and consistent public documentation and implementation plans.

The Business nucleus is the structured intake, scoping, delivery, service coordination, client lifecycle, DAO service request, and change management layer of Axodus.

## Business Thesis

Axodus Business is not only a commercial department.

Business is the operational runtime that converts requests into structured, reviewable, governable, and deliverable workflows.

Business receives and coordinates:

- client requests;
- DAO and sub-DAO service requests;
- custom governance plugin requests;
- product integration requests;
- technical delivery requests;
- Academy partner/course requests;
- Marketplace service requests;
- Trading product access requests;
- BBA campaign and positioning requests;
- automation and MCP requests;
- consulting and implementation requests.

Business is expected to be one of the largest consumers of ACS because every serious request requires classification, risk analysis, missing information detection, scoping, governance mapping, delivery planning, change request handling, acceptance, and accountability.

## Business Position in Axodus

Business sits between external demand and internal execution.

It translates:

- a client idea into a scoped service;
- a DAO need into a governance-aware request;
- a product opportunity into a delivery plan;
- a campaign need into a BBA workflow;
- a technical demand into an implementation task;
- a scope change into a transparent change request;
- a completed delivery into an acceptance and reporting record.

Business does not replace Governance.

Business routes governance-sensitive requests to Governance.

Business does not replace ACS.

Business consumes ACS outputs for analysis, classification, and operational intelligence.

Business does not replace human accountability.

Business owners validate scope, commitments, client communication, and delivery acceptance.

## Non-Negotiable Business Invariants

- Business is the structured intake and delivery runtime of Axodus.
- Business must not accept vague requests as executable commitments.
- Business must not bypass governance for governance-sensitive requests.
- Business must not promise unverified token, treasury, trading, or smart contract behavior.
- Business must document scope, deliverables, dependencies, risks, and acceptance criteria.
- Change requests must be logged, classified, and validated.
- Scope expansion must not be hidden inside execution.
- DAO service requests may require governance review.
- Custom governance plugins require technical, security, and governance review.
- ACS supports Business but does not approve commitments alone.
- BBA is a specialized blockchain business and advertising arm, not generic hype marketing.
- All public documentation must be in English.
