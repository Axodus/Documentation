## 1. DAO Service Thesis

DAOs and sub-DAOs may need specific tools, workflows, plugins, dashboards, automations, training, reports, or product access to operate within or alongside the Axodus ecosystem.

Business is the correct entry point for DAO service requests.

Business structures the request, ACS assists analysis, Governance validates sensitive items, and delivery teams implement approved scopes.

This prevents DAOs from receiving ad-hoc tools that bypass constitutional alignment, security, treasury discipline, or product access rules.

## 8. Public Documentation Guidance

The public DAO Service Requests page should:

- explain that DAOs can request services through Business;
- list request categories;
- explain intake fields;
- explain ACS and governance review;
- explain federation and constitutional alignment;
- explain risk flags;
- link to Governance, DAO Federation, Plugin Requests, ACS, and Business Runtime.
