## 1. Business and BBA Alignment Thesis

Marketplace can commercialize Business and BBA services, but custom work must remain scoped, delivered, and validated through Business runtime.

BBA supports Marketplace through listing copy, campaign strategy, product positioning, seller positioning, and claim review.

## 4. Service Request Flow

service_request_flow:
  - buyer_selects_or_requests_service
  - Marketplace_collects_initial_context
  - Business_classifies_scope
  - BBA_reviews_positioning_or_claims_if needed
  - quote_or_fixed_scope_confirmed
  - order_created
  - delivery_runtime_started
  - milestones_and_change_requests_tracked
  - acceptance_or_dispute_handled
  - settlement_and_report_completed

## 7. Public Documentation Guidance

The public Business and BBA Alignment page should:

- explain how Marketplace routes Business and BBA services;
- describe service request flow;
- list claim review triggers and risks;
- state that BBA is measurable but does not guarantee results.
