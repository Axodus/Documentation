## 1. Relationship Thesis

Academy is not isolated.

It depends on Governance for policy legitimacy, Business for structured partner/client/DAO educational requests, and ACS for analysis and operational intelligence.

Academy may become one of the first user-facing nuclei where Axodus education, token utility, marketplace commerce, and governance policy intersect.

Therefore, it needs clear boundaries between:

- product operation;
- business intake;
- AI-assisted review;
- governance approval;
- human accountability.

## 2. Governance Role in Academy

governance_role:
  - define_or_approve_high_impact_reward_policy
  - review_locked_reward_utility_rules
  - approve_tutor_quality_policy_if_ecosystem_level
  - define_marketplace_course_policy_if_needed
  - review_sensitive_course_categories
  - supervise_abuse_or_dispute_escalations
  - validate_certification_use_for_governance_or_product_access
  - publish_policy_changes_and_accountability_records

governance_required_when:
  - reward_budget_or_emission_policy_changes
  - free_vs_paid_reward_rules_change
  - locked_rewards_gain_governance_or_marketplace_utility
  - certification_affects_governance_rights_or_access
  - tutor_policy_affects_ecosystem_quality_or_sensitive_categories
  - partner_course_program_has_large_ecosystem_impact
  - marketplace_fee_distribution_changes_at_policy_level
  - abuse_incident_requires_public_accountability

governance_not_required_for:
  - routine_course_copy_edits
  - low-risk_course_metadata_updates
  - minor_UI_text_updates
  - ordinary_non-sensitive_course_publication_if_policy_allows
  - internal_quality_feedback_without_policy_change

## 3. Business Role in Academy

business_role:

  - coordinate_custom_course_development
  - structure_B2B_or_B2DAO_learning_programs
  - define_project_scope_and_deliverables
  - manage_client_or_partner_change_requests
  - route_governance_sensitive_items_to_governance

business_receives:
  - company_course_publishing_request
  - DAO_training_program_request
  - custom_Academy_track_request
  - tutor_partnership_request
  - sponsored_Learn_to_Win_campaign
  - BBA_education_campaign
  - certification_program_request
  - marketplace_course_bundle_request

business_outputs:
  - intake_form
  - request_classification
  - scope_document
  - deliverables
  - commercial_terms
  - governance_touchpoint_map
  - risk_classification
  - execution_plan

business_escalates_when:
  - request_changes_reward_policy
  - request_involves_$Neurons_distribution
  - request_requires_marketplace_fee_exception
  - request_requires_custom_certificate_with_access_power
  - request_affects_DAO_or_governance_participation
  - request_claims_official_Axodus_certification_status
  - request_involves_sensitive_topics_like_trading_or_tokenomics

## 5. Academy Policy Flow

academy_policy_flow:
  - policy_need_identified
  - Academy_or_Business_creates_policy_request
  - ACS_assists_analysis
  - risk_level_assigned
  - responsible_governance_layer_identified
  - human_review_performed
  - governance_review_or_vote_if_required
  - policy_approved_rejected_or_revised
  - Academy_runtime_updated
  - documentation_updated
  - accountability_record_created_if_material

## 8. Authority Boundaries

authority_boundaries:
  Academy:
    can:
      - operate_learning_paths
      - manage_course_content
      - define_course_structure_within_policy
      - track_progress
      - administer_assessments
    cannot:
      - unilaterally_change_high_impact_tokenomics
      - guarantee_token_value
      - bypass_tutor_policy
      - override_governance

  Business:
    can:
      - receive_and_scope_requests
      - coordinate_partner_programs
      - manage_delivery
      - route_escalations
    cannot:
      - approve_governance_sensitive_reward_policy_alone
      - promise_unverified_token_behavior
      - approve_sensitive_courses_without_review

  ACS:
    can:
      - analyze
      - summarize
      - detect_risks
      - recommend
    cannot:
      - final_approve
      - govern
      - mint_or_unlock
      - replace_human_review

  Governance:
    can:
      - approve_policy
      - supervise_high_impact_changes
      - define_constraints
      - require_accountability
    cannot:
      - ignore_contract_security
      - guarantee_outcomes
      - replace_educational_quality_work

## 9. Public Documentation Guidance

Public Academy governance-alignment docs should:

- explain when Academy changes need governance;
- show how Business handles partner and DAO requests;
- explain ACS support role;
- define authority boundaries;
- link Academy to Governance, Business, ACS, Marketplace, and Tokenomics;
- avoid implying full automation or final policy where not implemented.
