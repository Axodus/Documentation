## 1. Definition

Learn-to-Win is the gamified education and reward model of Axodus Academy.

It is designed to reward meaningful learning, progression, skill acquisition, and ecosystem onboarding.

Learn-to-Win should not reward empty clicks, low-effort farming, or purely speculative behavior.

The model must connect learning outcomes to measurable progression and carefully governed rewards.

## 6. Free Course Reward Logic

free_course_rewards:
  principle:
    - free_courses_can_onboard_users_and_may_issue_smaller_rewards
    - rewards_from_free_courses_may_be_locked_or_internal_use_only
    - free_course_rewards_should_not_be_immediately_dumpable_if_the_policy_requires_sustainability

possible_behavior:
  - locked_balance
  - internal_platform_credit
  - marketplace_spendable_balance
  - governance_participation_weight_if_supported
  - product_access_signal
  - non_transferable_until_conditions_met_if_contract_supports

documentation_warning:
  - Do not claim free rewards are non-transferable unless contract implementation enforces it.
  - Do not claim free rewards can be used in Marketplace or voting unless supported or marked as planned.
  - Always distinguish design intent from implemented behavior.

## 7. Paid Course Reward Logic

paid_course_rewards:
  principle:
    - paid_course_rewards_may_have_different_unlock_or_transfer_rules
    - paid_course_purchase_may_reduce_abuse_risk_but_does_not_eliminate_it
    - rewards_should_still_depend_on_real_completion_or_validation

possible_behavior:
  - unlocked_rewards
  - partially_locked_rewards
  - higher_reward_rate
  - certification_related_rewards
  - premium_learning_path_access

documentation_warning:
  - Do not claim paid-course rewards are automatically liquid unless final token contracts and policy confirm it.
  - State that paid-course reward behavior depends on finalized policy and implementation.

## 12. Learn-to-Win Anti-Patterns

anti_patterns:
  reward_first_design:
    problem:
      - learning_becomes_secondary_to_extraction

  no_assessment_rewards:
    problem:
      - rewards_can_be_farmed_without_knowledge

  unlimited_free_course_rewards:
    problem:
      - tokenomics_and_abuse_risk

  vague_reward_rules:
    problem:
      - users_misunderstand_eligibility_and_utility

  immediate_liquid_rewards_for_trivial_tasks:
    problem:
      - creates_sell_pressure_and_abuse

  hidden_reward_changes:
    problem:
      - damages_trust_and_accountability

## 13. Public Documentation Guidance

The public Learn-to-Win page should:

- explain the model in plain English;
- emphasize learning and risk education;
- explain milestones, assessments, rewards, and certification;
- explain free vs paid course reward differences carefully;
- include anti-abuse considerations;
- link to Proof of Knowledge, Token Reward Flows, Locked Rewards, and Marketplace Integration;
- avoid financial or speculative reward language.
