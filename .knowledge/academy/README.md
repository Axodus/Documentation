## Purpose

This folder contains the deep semantic knowledge base for the Academy nucleus of Axodus.

These files are not public-facing documentation pages. They are agent-oriented knowledge packs designed to help documentation agents, coding agents, governance agents, ACS agents, and future MCP systems generate accurate and consistent public documentation for the Academy nucleus.

The Academy nucleus is one of the central onboarding, education, reputation, and token utility layers of Axodus. It connects learning, user progression, Proof of Knowledge, Learn-to-Win, certifications, tutor/course publishing, Marketplace commerce, and `$Neurons` reward logic.

## Academy Thesis

Axodus Academy is not only a content library.

It is an ecosystem-level education engine that can:

- onboard users into Axodus products;
- teach users how to safely interact with DeFi, trading, governance, and Web3 tools;
- validate learning through Proof of Knowledge;
- reward participation through Learn-to-Win mechanisms;
- support locked/internal-use token rewards for free educational flows;
- support paid course economics and tutor revenue models;
- connect educational products to the Marketplace;
- create reputation, certification, and access pathways across the ecosystem;
- support governance education and informed community participation.

The Academy must be documented carefully because it touches token rewards, user incentives, potential abuse/farming risks, marketplace commerce, tutor validation, and governance participation.

## How Agents Should Use This Pack

Agents must use this pack as contextual source material when generating or reviewing Academy documentation.

When generating public pages, agents should:

- write final documentation in English;
- preserve the Academy's role as a central onboarding and education nucleus;
- distinguish implemented features from planned mechanisms;
- avoid claiming final smart contract behavior unless verified;
- explain free-course locked rewards carefully;
- explain paid-course reward/unlock behavior as dependent on finalized contracts and policy;
- describe `$Neurons` as utility, reward, access, and governance participation infrastructure without investment language;
- connect Academy to Governance, Business, Marketplace, Tokenomics, ACS, Security, and Accountability;
- include anti-abuse, anti-sybil, quality control, and risk considerations.

## Non-Negotiable Academy Invariants

- Academy is a central onboarding and education nucleus.
- Academy is connected to token utility but must not be documented as an investment system.
- Free-course rewards may be locked or internally usable depending on contract implementation.
- Paid-course rewards may follow different unlock/reward rules.
- Contract behavior must not be invented.
- Tutor and course publishing require validation and quality controls.
- Learn-to-Win must include anti-abuse and anti-farming considerations.
- Proof of Knowledge must be meaningful and resistant to superficial completion.
- Marketplace integration must preserve clear commerce, fee, royalty, and access rules.
- Governance may be required for reward policy, tutor standards, marketplace policy, or high-impact Academy rules.
- ACS may assist review, classification, content analysis, and risk detection but must not be final authority.
