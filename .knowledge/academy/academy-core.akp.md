## 1. Academy Identity

academy_role:
  primary:
    - education_nucleus
    - onboarding_engine
    - user_progression_layer
    - Proof_of_Knowledge_runtime
    - Learn_to_Win_runtime
    - token_utility_activation_layer
    - certification_and_reputation_layer
    - marketplace_connected_course_economy

academy_is_not:
  - simple_video_library
  - generic_LMS_only
  - investment_platform
  - guaranteed_reward_generator
  - unrestricted_token_minting_machine
  - unreviewed_course_marketplace
  - replacement_for_governance_or_security_training

academy_thesis:
  - Axodus Academy is the educational entry point for users, builders, contributors, DAO participants, and ecosystem clients.
  - The Academy should explain how Axodus works and how users can safely interact with DeFi, governance, trading, token utility, and marketplace flows.
  - The Academy can distribute rewards, but reward behavior must be contract-controlled and documented with caution.
  - The Academy can serve as a reputation and certification layer for governance participation, marketplace trust, tutor quality, and user progression.
  - Academy is deeply connected to `$Neurons`, but `$Neurons` must be described as utility, reward, access, and governance participation infrastructure, not as an investment promise.

mission:

  - support_tutor_and_course_economy
  - connect_courses_to_marketplace_commerce
  - create_reputation_and_certification_pathways

  - reduce_user_risk_through_education

mission_statement:
  "Axodus Academy provides structured education, onboarding, knowledge validation, and token utility pathways for the Axodus ecosystem, connecting learning, rewards, reputation, governance, and marketplace activity through transparent and risk-aware mechanisms."

## 3. Academy Principles

principles:
  education_first:
    meaning:

  transparency:

      - course_terms_must_be_clear

  quality:
    meaning:
      - tutor_courses_should_be_reviewed
      - course_claims_should_be_checked

      - Academy_policies_may_require_governance_review
      - reward_policy_may_require_governance

  utility_without_speculation:
    meaning:
      - `$Neurons`_may_be_used_for_access_rewards_voting_or_marketplace_use
      - token_language_must_not_imply_guaranteed_price_or_yield

## 10. Academy Anti-Patterns

anti_patterns:
  reward_farming_over_learning:
    problem:
      - users_complete_low_quality_tasks_only_to_extract_rewards

  free_rewards_as_liquid_profit:
    problem:
      - creates_speculative_and_abuse_risk

  unclear_locked_balance:
    problem:
      - users_misunderstand_transferability_and_utility

  tutor_approval_without_review:
    problem:
      - low_quality_or_harmful_courses_enter_ecosystem

  marketplace_without_fee_clarity:
    problem:
      - tutors_users_and_platform_do_not_understand_economic_distribution

  Proof_of_Knowledge_as_simple_click_completion:
    problem:
      - credentials_become_meaningless

  AI_auto_approval_of_courses:
    problem:
      - accountability_removed_from_quality_control

  investment_language_for_rewards:
    problem:
      - legal_reputational_and_user_trust_risk

  claiming_final_contract_behavior:
    problem:
      - docs_become_inaccurate_if_contracts_change

## 11. Public Documentation Guidance

When creating public Academy docs:

- Explain Academy as a core education and onboarding layer.
- Explain Learn-to-Win and Proof of Knowledge separately.
- Explain free and paid course reward flows carefully.
- Explain locked rewards as a contract-controlled concept.
- Include tutor/course publishing models.
- Include marketplace relationship.
- Include governance and ACS touchpoints.
- Include anti-abuse and risk notes.
- Mark incomplete mechanisms as Draft, Planned, or Needs Review.
