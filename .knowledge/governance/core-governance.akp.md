## 1. Governance Identity

governance_role:
  primary:
    - coordination_heart_of_Axodus
    - constitutional_alignment_layer
    - ecosystem_decision_runtime
    - DAO_federation_authority
    - treasury_supervision_interface
    - public_accountability_trigger
    - product_access_coordination_layer

governance_is_not:
  - a_marketing_label
  - a_simple_voting_page
  - a_disconnected_DAO_feature
  - a_token_price_narrative
  - an_unrestricted_majority_rule_system
  - an_AI_autopilot

governance_thesis:
  - Axodus requires governance because it is not a single product.
  - Axodus contains multiple nuclei with different risk profiles, financial dependencies, execution surfaces, and community stakeholders.
  - Governance provides the shared coordination layer that keeps these nuclei aligned.
  - Governance must protect sustainability, transparency, and accountability while allowing local DAO experimentation.
  - Governance must create auditable pathways between proposals, decisions, execution, treasury actions, product access, and public reporting.

## 2. Governance Mission

mission:
  - coordinate ecosystem decisions
  - validate strategic direction
  - supervise treasury-sensitive actions
  - enable DAO federation
  - enforce constitutional principles
  - support public accountability
  - create traceability between intent, vote, execution, and result

mission_statement:
  "Axodus Governance coordinates strategic, technical, financial, and community decisions across the ecosystem through a layered DAO model, constitutional guardrails, federated DAO participation, and accountable execution flows."

## 5. Governance Layer Summary

layers:
  Executive_DAO:
    semantic_role:
      - strategic_direction
      - core_development_coordination
      - roadmap_alignment
      - technical_prioritization
      - executive_sponsorship

  Boardroom_Council:
    semantic_role:
      - supervisory_review
      - constitutional_validation
      - risk_filtering
      - treasury_oversight
      - compliance_alignment
      - escalation_layer

  Community_DAO:
    semantic_role:
      - broad_participation
      - public_voting
      - ecosystem_signaling
      - community_legitimacy
      - decentralized_feedback

layering_logic:
  - Not every proposal has the same risk.
  - Low-risk proposals may require lightweight community signaling.
  - High-risk proposals may require Boardroom review.
  - Strategic proposals may require Executive DAO sponsorship or validation.
  - Treasury, protocol, plugin, and constitutional proposals require stronger controls.
  - Governance must remain legible and proportional to risk.

## 9. Critical Invariants

critical_invariants:
  - Governance must remain the coordination heart of Axodus.
  - Governance must not be reduced to a simple voting UI.
  - Constitutional alignment is required for official federation.
  - Local autonomy must not bypass shared rules.
  - Treasury decisions require visibility and reporting.
  - Plugin requests must be reviewed before official integration.
  - ACS analysis is advisory unless governance explicitly authorizes an action path.
  - No documentation should state that a governance feature is live unless verified in the implementation.
  - Proposal execution must produce an accountability trail.
  - Risk must be explicit for treasury, trading, tokenomics, and smart contract changes.
