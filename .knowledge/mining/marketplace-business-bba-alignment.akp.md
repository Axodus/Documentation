## 1. Alignment Thesis

Mining can be commercialized through Marketplace and Business, but mining-related services and products are claim-sensitive.

BBA must review public claims to prevent guaranteed ROI, passive income, or unsupported sustainability/performance claims.

## 7. Public Documentation Guidance

The public Marketplace, Business and BBA Alignment page should:

- explain Marketplace listings and Business service interfaces;
- define BBA claim review role;
- list claim review triggers and risks;
- state no guaranteed mining outcomes.
