## 1. Education Thesis

Mining is often misunderstood as passive income.

Academy must teach users that mining depends on technical, operational, market, energy, and regulatory factors.

User-facing mining access should require appropriate education and risk acknowledgement.

## 5. Public Documentation Guidance

The public Academy and User Education page should:

- explain why mining education matters;
- list education topics;
- define access education requirements;
- include user confirmation fields;
- link to Academy, Risk, Profitability, and User Products.
