## 1. Sustainability Thesis

Mining can consume significant energy and create heat, noise, hardware waste, and local operational impacts.

Axodus Mining should document energy and sustainability carefully without unsupported green claims.

## 4. Prohibited Sustainability Claims

prohibited_claims:
  - carbon_neutral_without_evidence
  - renewable_powered_without_evidence
  - zero_impact
  - green_mining_guaranteed
  - environmentally_risk_free
  - sustainable_without_defined_basis
  - offset_claim_without_source

## 5. Safe Language

safe_language:
  - energy_profile
  - efficiency-focused
  - subject_to_energy_source_verification
  - sustainability_review_pending
  - responsible_operations
  - energy_usage_estimate
  - evidence-based_sustainability_claim
  - active_if_verified

## 6. Public Documentation Guidance

The public Sustainability and Responsible Operations page should:

- explain energy, efficiency, heat, noise, and hardware lifecycle considerations;
- define sustainability record fields;
- list prohibited and safe language;
- avoid unsupported environmental claims.
