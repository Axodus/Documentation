## 2. Core Thesis

thesis:
  - Axodus Mining is the infrastructure and product nucleus for mining-related operations, reward accounting, monitoring, user education, treasury exposure, and marketplace/service integration.
  - Mining can mean Proof-of-Work mining, hashrate-based operations, hosted mining, mining pool participation, or node/validator operations depending on chain model.
  - Mining rewards are variable and uncertain. They depend on network, hardware, energy, pool, uptime, market, operational, validator, and regulatory conditions.
  - Axodus Mining must be documented as a risk-aware operational system, not as guaranteed passive income.
  - Any user-facing mining product must clearly define ownership, allocation, payout rules, fees, costs, restrictions, and risk.
