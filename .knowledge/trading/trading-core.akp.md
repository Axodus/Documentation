## 1. Trading Identity

trading_role:
  primary:
    - internal_financial_operations_layer
    - trading_strategy_research_layer
    - revenue_generation_support_layer
    - user_facing_strategy_product_layer
    - risk_management_layer
    - trading_education_and_onboarding_layer
    - market_intelligence_layer

trading_is_not:
  - guaranteed_profit_system
  - passive_income_promise
  - financial_advice_engine
  - risk_free_bot_platform
  - unrestricted_treasury_trader
  - custody_solution_by_default
  - exchange_or_broker
  - replacement_for_user_judgment
  - replacement_for_governance_or_treasury_review

trading_thesis:
  - Axodus Trading is responsible for developing and operating risk-aware trading capabilities that may support ecosystem revenue, treasury strategy, user-facing tools, and product activation.
  - Trading is a major product and financial nucleus, but it must be documented with strong risk controls.
  - Trading can help users start with small amounts and learn how strategies work, but this must never be described as guaranteed income.
  - Trading tools should be connected to Academy education, Tokenomics access rules, Business delivery, Treasury reporting, Governance review, and ACS/Trinity analysis.

## 3. Trading Principles

principles:
  risk_first:
    meaning:
      - every_strategy_and_product_must_include_risk_language
      - leverage_and_futures_require_stronger_disclosure

  no_guarantees:
    meaning:
      - no_strategy_guarantees_profit
      - backtests_do_not_guarantee_future_performance
      - past_results_do_not_guarantee_future_results

  user_control:
    meaning:
      - user_facing_strategies_should_run_in_user_owned_accounts_unless_explicitly_defined
      - users_retain_responsibility_for_capital_account_risk_and_API_keys

  no_custody_by_default:
    meaning:
      - Axodus_should_not_hold_user_funds_for_user_facing_CEX_API_strategies_by_default

  least_privilege_API:
    meaning:
      - exchange_API_keys_should_disable_withdrawals
      - permissions_should_be_limited_to_required_actions

  governance_alignment:
    meaning:
      - treasury_trading_and_material_strategy_access_policy_require_governance_visibility_or_approval

  reporting:
    meaning:
      - strategy_results_risk_drawdown_and_incidents_should_be_reported_with_context

  education_before_access:
    meaning:
      - users_should_understand_strategy_risk_before_using_trading_tools
      - Academy_paths_can_prepare_users

  product_activation:
    meaning:
      - selected_strategies_can_be_used_as_entry_products_to_create_user_engagement
      - promotional_access_must_not_imply_profit

## 9. Critical Invariants

## 10. Public Documentation Guidance

Public Trading docs should:

- clearly define internal vs user-facing trading;
- explain trading as risk-managed product and financial operation;
- document user responsibility and no-custody assumptions;
- explain API key security;
- describe strategy lifecycle and access model;
- connect Trading to Treasury, Governance, Academy, Tokenomics, ACS, and Accountability;
- avoid hype or guaranteed profit language.
