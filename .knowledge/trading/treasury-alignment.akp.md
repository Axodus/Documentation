## 1. Treasury Alignment Thesis

Trading is expected to be a major financial management and revenue generation nucleus for Axodus.

However, treasury-related trading must be disciplined, transparent, risk-limited, governance-aware, and reportable.

Trading should support Treasury sustainability, not create opaque financial exposure.

## 2. Treasury Trading Objectives

objectives:
  - generate_consistent_ecosystem_revenue_where_possible
  - support_treasury_growth_under_risk_limits
  - test_strategies_before_productization
  - provide_market_intelligence
  - support_liquidity_or_market_operations_if approved
  - produce_financial_reports_for_governance_and_accountability

## 9. Public Documentation Guidance

The public Treasury Alignment page should:

- explain Trading as potential revenue generation and financial management support;
- define treasury trading policy components;
- list strategy categories with risk caveats;
- explain review and reporting requirements;
- include strong risk language;
- link to Treasury, Governance, Risk Management, and Accountability.
