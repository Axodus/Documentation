## 1. Internal Trading Thesis

Internal Trading is the Axodus-facing trading function that may support ecosystem revenue, treasury strategy analysis, liquidity operations, market intelligence, and financial sustainability.

Internal Trading is not uncontrolled treasury speculation.

Any internal trading involving ecosystem capital must be governed by risk limits, treasury policy, reporting, and accountability.

## 2. Internal Trading Objectives

objectives:
  - support_ecosystem_revenue_generation
  - research_market_opportunities
  - support_treasury_strategy
  - evaluate_strategy_viability_before_user_facing_release
  - produce_market_intelligence
  - test_execution_infrastructure
  - develop_risk_management_practices
  - provide_data_for_governance_and_treasury_reports

modes:
  research_only:

  paper_trading:
    description:
      - simulated_execution_against_live_market_data

  limited_capital_pilot:
    description:
      - controlled_live_capital_test_under_limits

  treasury_strategy:
    description:
      - approved_treasury_trading_strategy_with_reporting

  market_making_or_liquidity_support:
    description:
      - strategy_intended_to_support_liquidity_if_governance_and_treasury approve

  arbitrage_or_opportunity_strategy:
    description:
      - controlled_strategy_to_capture_market_inefficiencies_under_limits

  trend_or_signal_strategy:
    description:
      - strategy_based_on_market_direction_or_signals

## 4. Treasury Capital Rules

treasury_capital_rules:
  - live_treasury_capital_requires_governance_or_treasury_authority
  - strategy_must_have_risk_limits
  - leverage_requires_heightened_review
  - losses_must_be_reported
  - performance_must_be_contextualized
  - capital_allocation_must_be_recorded

## 8. Internal Trading Risks

risks:
  treasury_loss:
    description:
      - strategy_loses_ecosystem_capital

  uncontrolled_leverage:
    description:
      - leverage_amplifies_losses_or_liquidation

  exchange_failure:
    description:
      - CEX_downtime_API_failure_or_account_restriction

  model_overfitting:
    description:
      - strategy_performed_in_backtest_but_fails_live

  liquidity_risk:
    description:
      - insufficient_market_depth_for_execution

  reporting_gap:
    description:
      - strategy_results_not_visible_to_governance_or_treasury

  governance_bypass:
    description:
      - live_capital_deployed_without_approval

## 9. Public Documentation Guidance

The public Internal Trading page should:

- define internal trading separately from user-facing strategies;
- explain objectives and modes;
- explain treasury capital rules;
- define review flow and metrics;
- explain governance and reporting requirements;
- include strong risk language.
