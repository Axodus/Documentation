## 1. Disclosure Thesis

DeFi documentation must be useful without becoming investment advice or financial promotion.

Axodus must avoid regulated or guaranteed language unless reviewed and supported.

## 2. Required Disclosure Themes

disclosure_themes:
  no_financial_advice:
    message:
      - DeFi_docs_are_for_information_education_and_product_context_not_personal_financial_advice

  no_yield_guarantee:
    message:
      - APY_yield_rewards_and_returns_are_variable_and_not guaranteed

  smart_contract_risk:
    message:
      - contracts_and_protocols_can_have_bugs_or_be_exploited

  market_risk:
    message:
      - asset_values_can_change_and_users_can_lose_value

  liquidity_risk:
    message:
      - withdrawals_swaps_or_exits_may_be_delayed_or_impacted_by_slippage

  protocol_risk:
    message:
      - external_protocols_can_fail_change_or_be_attacked

  regulatory_uncertainty:
    message:
      - DeFi_products_may_be_subject_to_uncertain_or_changing_rules

  ETF_language:
    message:
      - ETF_DaaS_is_ETF_oriented_design_language_not_a_regulated_ETF_unless legally established

## 3. Prohibited Claims

prohibited_claims:
  - guaranteed_APY
  - guaranteed_yield
  - guaranteed_profit
  - risk_free_DeFi
  - safe_yield
  - fixed_return_unless_legally_and_contractually_defined
  - regulated_ETF_without_legal_basis
  - audited_without_audit
  - principal_protected_without_basis
  - passive_income_without_risk
  - legally_compliant_without_review

## 4. Safe Language

safe_language:
  - variable_yield
  - estimated_APY
  - subject_to_market_and_protocol_risk
  - subject_to_governance_approval
  - subject_to_security_review
  - planned
  - draft
  - active_if_verified
  - ETF-oriented
  - risk-aware
  - reportable
  - educational

## 5. Review Triggers

review_triggers:
  - public_DeFi_product_page
  - APY_or_yield_claim
  - ETF_DaaS_page
  - derivatives_or_debentures_page
  - staking_launch
  - vault_launch
  - liquidity_incentive_campaign
  - treasury_DeFi_strategy_report
  - security_or_audit_claim
  - legal_or_compliance_claim

## 6. Public Documentation Guidance

The public Compliance and Disclosures page should:

- state no financial advice and no guaranteed yield;
- define required disclosure themes;
- list prohibited and safe language;
- explain review triggers;
- avoid legal conclusions.
