## 1. Sensitivity Thesis

Derivatives and debentures are high-sensitivity financial concepts.

They may be part of Axodus long-term DeFi, Treasury, or DaaS strategy discussions, but they must not be documented as active products unless legally, technically, governance, treasury, and risk validated.

Public documentation must treat them as Draft, Planned, Conceptual, or Needs Review unless confirmed.

## 2. Definitions

derivatives:
  definition:
    - financial_instruments_whose_value_depends_on_underlying_asset_index_rate_event_or_condition

debentures:
  definition:

## 5. Prohibited Claims

prohibited_claims:
  - fixed_return_unless_legally_and_contractually_defined
  - guaranteed_income
  - risk_free_debt
  - regulated_product_without_legal_status
  - principal_protected_unless_legally_and_contractually_true
  - ETF_or_security_status_without_basis
  - public_sale_without_review

## 7. Public Documentation Guidance

The public Derivatives and Debentures page should:

- define terms cautiously;
- mark status as Conceptual/Draft/Needs Review unless verified;
- explain required reviews;
- list prohibited claims and risks;
- avoid launching or marketing these as active products without authorization.
