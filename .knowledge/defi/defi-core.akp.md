## 2. Core Thesis

thesis:
  - Axodus DeFi is a structured decentralized finance layer designed around service-oriented products, governance alignment, risk management, treasury visibility, smart contract security, education, and accountability.
  - Axodus DeFi must avoid speculative or hype-driven yield language.
  - DeFi strategies may generate returns, losses, fees, slippage, lockups, or exposure. These outcomes must be reported honestly and never guaranteed.
  - DaaS is the main operating direction: DeFi as a Service.
  - ETF DaaS is a strategic design direction for diversified, ETF-oriented DeFi exposure, but it must not be described as a regulated ETF unless legally established.
