## 1. Growth Thesis

BBA growth operations are designed to improve acquisition, activation, retention, conversion, and trust through measurable experiments and clear communication.

Growth is not manipulation.

Growth must respect product truth, user safety, governance alignment, and claim review.

## 2. Growth Funnel

## 6. Growth Rules

growth_rules:
  - define_goal_before_campaign
  - define_metric_before_launch
  - do_not_optimize_for_misleading_clicks
  - do_not_hide_risk_to_increase_conversion
  - do_not_use_fake_urgency
  - do_not_publish_unreviewed_claims
  - measure_quality_not_only_volume
  - document_experiments_and_lessons

## 7. Public Documentation Guidance

The public Growth Operations page should:

- explain growth funnel and levers;
- define metrics and experiments;
- explain growth rules;
- state that BBA is measurable but does not guarantee results.
