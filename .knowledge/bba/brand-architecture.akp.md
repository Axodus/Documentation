[BBA_BRAND_ARCHITECTURE]

## 1. Brand Architecture Thesis

BBA must help Axodus and clients maintain coherent brand systems.

Axodus is an ecosystem with many nuclei and products. Without brand architecture, products become fragmented and users cannot understand how they relate.

## 2. Axodus Brand Hierarchy

brand_hierarchy:
  Axodus:
    role:
      - parent_ecosystem

  Axodus_Core:
    role:
      - strategic_governance_and_foundational_layer

  Axodus_Business:
    role:
      - business_services_delivery_runtime_and_commercial_coordination

  Axodus_BBA:
    role:
      - specialized_business_and_advertising_agency_under_Business

  Product_Nuclei:
    examples:
      - Academy
      - Marketplace
      - Trading
      - Treasury
      - Governance
      - ACS
      - DEX
      - DeFi
      - Lottery
      - Mining

  Client_or_DAO_Brands:
    role:
      - external_or_partner_brand_supported_by_BBA

## 3. Naming Rules

naming_rules:
  - use_Axodus_BBA_as_primary_name
  - define_BBA_as_Blockchain_Business_and_Advertising_on_first_use
  - do_not_use_BBA_as_generic_advertising_only
  - preserve_product_brand_hierarchy
  - avoid_creating_unapproved_subbrands
  - keep_naming_simple_and_explainable
  - distinguish_Axodus_internal_products_from_client_projects

## 4. Visual Identity Responsibilities

visual_identity_scope:
  - brand_briefs
  - product_family_alignment
  - campaign_visual_direction
  - landing_page_design_direction
  - logo_usage_guidance_if_provided
  - visual_hierarchy
  - tag_systems
  - product_badges
  - Figma_ready_tokens_if_requested

visual_identity_boundaries:
  - BBA_can_prepare_design_direction
  - final_design_may_require_designer_review
  - do_not_share_font_files
  - do_not_claim_final_brand_system_without_approval

## 5. Messaging Architecture

messaging_architecture:
  core_message:
    definition:
      - one_sentence_explaining_product_value

  supporting_messages:
    definition:
      - proof_points_or_use_cases

  trust_messages:
    definition:
      - governance_security_risk_accountability_language

  CTA:
    definition:
      - next_action_user_should_take

  disclaimer_or_note:
    definition:
      - risk_or_status_caveat_when_needed

## 6. Brand Risks

risks:
  brand_fragmentation:
    description:
      - products_look_or_sound_unrelated

  claim_inconsistency:
    description:
      - different_materials_make_conflicting_claims

  hype_dilution:
    description:
      - serious_brand_credibility_damaged_by_pump_language

  client_confusion:
    description:
      - users_cannot_tell_Axodus_product_from_client_project

  outdated_assets:
    description:
      - old_logo_copy_or_positioning_used_after_change

## 7. Public Documentation Guidance

The public Brand Architecture page should:

- explain BBA's role in brand coherence;
- define hierarchy;
- list naming rules;
- explain messaging architecture;
- include risks and boundaries.
