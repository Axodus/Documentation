## 2. Core Thesis

thesis:
  - Axodus BBA exists because blockchain products require communication that is technically accurate, business-aware, user-friendly, and responsible.
  - Web3 products often fail because users cannot understand them or because marketing overpromises what the product cannot safely claim.
  - BBA translates technical, governance, tokenomics, DeFi, DAO, Academy, Marketplace, Trading, and Treasury context into clear public narratives.
  - BBA must support growth without compromising trust.
  - BBA is ROI-aware, but it must never guarantee ROI.

## 6. Relationship with Business

business_relationship:
  - BBA_is_a_specialized_service_line_inside_Axodus_Business.
  - Business handles intake, scoping, commercial structure, client lifecycle, delivery coordination, change requests, and acceptance.
  - BBA handles communication strategy, campaign planning, claim review, content, launch support, and growth execution inside the approved Business runtime.
  - BBA should not accept unscoped work outside Business runtime for material client or DAO requests.
