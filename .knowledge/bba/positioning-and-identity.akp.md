## 1. Positioning Thesis

Axodus BBA positions itself as a serious blockchain-native business and advertising agency.

The key differentiator is the combination of:

- business strategy;
- Web3 literacy;
- claim review;
- AI-assisted operations;
- governance awareness;
- product understanding;
- transparent reporting;
- growth discipline.

BBA should be positioned as the opposite of hype-driven crypto marketing.

## 2. Positioning Statement

positioning_statement:
  "Axodus BBA is a blockchain-native business and advertising agency that helps Web3 products, DAOs, creators, and companies communicate clearly, launch responsibly, and grow with measurable, claim-reviewed strategies."

## 3. Value Proposition

value_proposition:
  - Translate_complex_Web3_products_into_clear_business_narratives.
  - Build campaigns that are measurable without making unrealistic promises.
  - Protect brands from unsafe financial, token, trading, audit, partnership, and governance claims.
  - Connect communication with real product strategy, onboarding, and conversion.
  - Use ACS agents to accelerate research, drafting, review, and analysis under human validation.
  - Align client communication with Business runtime and governance-sensitive requirements.
