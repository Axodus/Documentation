## 1. Narrative Thesis

Narrative is the structure that helps users understand what a product is, why it matters, how it works, what risks exist, and what action to take next.

BBA narrative should be clear, educational, and grounded in product truth.

## 2. Narrative Layers

      - explain_specific_product_value_and_usage

  user_problem_narrative:

## 4. Narrative Quality Rules

quality_rules:
  - define_audience
  - define_user_problem
  - state_product_status
  - avoid_overclaiming
  - explain_risks_when_relevant
  - include_next_action
  - avoid_jargon_unless_audience_is_technical
  - link_to_docs_for_complex_claims
  - preserve_consistent_terms_from_Glossary

## 5. Content Review Checklist

review_checklist:
  - Is_the_content_in_English_for_public_docs?
  - Does_it_match_current_product_status?
  - Are_claims_supported?
  - Are_financial_or_trading_claims_safe?
  - Are_tokenomics_claims_utility_focused?
  - Are_risk_disclosures_present_when_needed?
  - Is_the_call_to_action_clear?
  - Is_the_tone_professional_and_transparent?
  - Does_it_align_with_Axodus_brand_and_governance?

## 6. Public Documentation Guidance

The public Content and Narrative page should:

- explain narrative layers;
- list content types;
- define quality rules;
- provide review checklist;
- connect narrative to claim review and glossary consistency.
