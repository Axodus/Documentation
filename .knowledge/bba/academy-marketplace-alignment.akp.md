## 1. Alignment Thesis

BBA supports Academy and Marketplace by making educational products, tutor offerings, service listings, and marketplace items understandable and commercially viable.

BBA must also protect users from misleading reward, token, course, certification, or seller claims.

## 2. Academy Support

Academy_support:
  services:
    - course_positioning
    - learning_path_copy
    - tutor_profile_positioning
    - course_launch_campaign
    - Academy_onboarding_content
    - Proof_of_Knowledge_explainer
    - Learn_to_Win_reward_language_review

Academy_claim_risks:
  - guaranteed_income_from_learning
  - guaranteed_certification_value
  - transferable_rewards_without_contract_support
  - professional_certification_claim_without_basis
  - tutor_quality_claim_without_review

## 3. Marketplace Support

Marketplace_support:
  services:
    - listing_copy
    - seller_or_tutor_positioning
    - service_catalog_copy
    - product_page_copy
    - marketplace_launch_campaign
    - category_messaging
    - conversion_optimization

Marketplace_claim_risks:
  - false_seller_credential
  - unclear_refund_policy
  - unsupported_payment_claim
  - locked_rewards_spend_claim_without_policy
  - guaranteed_sales_for_sellers
  - misleading_price_or_discount

listing_copy_fields:

## 5. Reward and Token Language

reward_token_language_rules:
  - describe_$Neurons_as_utility_not_investment
  - describe_locked_rewards_as_restricted_or_internal_use_if_supported
  - do_not_claim_free_course_rewards_can_be_sold
  - do_not_claim_marketplace_accepts_locked_rewards_without_policy
  - do_not_claim_course_purchase_generates_profit
  - explain_reward_eligibility_not_guaranteed_income

## 6. Public Documentation Guidance

The public Academy and Marketplace Alignment page should:

- explain how BBA supports Academy and Marketplace;
- list services;
- define listing copy fields;
- explain reward/token language rules;
- include claim risks.
