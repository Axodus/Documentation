# AXODUS COMMERCIAL CORE

Version: 0.1 Draft  
Status: Commercial Strategy

---

## 1. Overview

Axodus is not positioned as a conventional Web3 app.

Axodus is a governed economic ecosystem where DAOs, products, agents, services and treasury operations create sustainable value.

---

## 2. Commercial Thesis

The product is the marketing.

Axodus should grow through:

- working products;
- documented performance;
- real revenue;
- governance transparency;
- education;
- DAO-driven expansion;
- partner operators;
- practical utility.

---

## 3. Primary Customer Types

### Investor / Holder
Seeks exposure to ecosystem revenue, treasury growth and long-term participation.

### DAO Operator
Creates and manages a DAO, clients, treasury and product allocations.

### Commercial Agent / Investment Agent
Brings clients and receives commissions or revenue share through DAO structure.

### Student / User
Begins through Academy, earns $Neurons and accesses products.

### Business Client
Consumes BBA, governance, development, marketing or infrastructure services.

### Compute Provider
Provides GPU/LLM processing capacity.

## 5. DAO-as-a-Business

The main commercial innovation is enabling individuals, companies and groups to operate their own DAOs inside Axodus.

Each DAO may:

- onboard clients;
- manage treasury;
- allocate capital;
- access products;
- use agents;
- earn revenue;
- distribute shares.

---

## 6. BBA Positioning

Axodus BBA is an AI-operated advertising and business agency specialized in blockchain and tokenized businesses.

It belongs to both:

- Business layer;
- MCP / Agent execution layer.

---

## 7. Commercial Expansion

Axodus expands through:

- partner DAOs;
- agent-led operations;
- marketplace listings;
- Academy funnels;
- BBA clients;
- documented trading ignition;
- governance adoption.

---

## 8. Final Principle

Axodus should not depend on hype. It should become commercially relevant because its products work and its governance creates trust.
