# AXODUS TRADING PRINCIPLES

Version: 0.1 Draft  
Status: Trading Doctrine

---

## 1. Core Statement

The Trading Core is the ignition layer of the Axodus ecosystem.

Its role is to generate initial operational fuel, validate economic efficiency, document real performance and eventually provide practical product access to users.

Trading is not the final destination of Axodus. It is the first engine used to start the broader ecosystem machine.

---

## 2. Strategic Role

Trading Core serves as:

- internal capital ignition;
- product validation;
- operational proof;
- marketing through documented performance;
- gateway product for users;
- education-to-utility bridge;
- treasury growth engine.

---

## 3. Internal First Rule

No public trading product should launch before it works internally.

The strategy must first operate for:

- the founder/dev;
- internal Axodus operators;
- controlled capital;
- documented conditions;
- limited risk.

Only after documented efficiency should it become a public-facing product.

---

## 4. Risk Philosophy

- Preservation first.
- No unrealistic APR promises.
- No uncontrolled leverage.
- No user access without education.
- No API withdrawal permissions.
- No scaling without documented performance.
- No strategy promotion without risk disclosure.

---

## 5. Public Gateway Product

The first public trading product may be a limited API-based bot.

The user:

- keeps capital in their own exchange account;
- connects a restricted API key;
- uses a low capital limit;
- accesses through NFT license;
- must complete Academy requirements;
- receives ACS guidance.

---

## 6. Free / Low-Cost Tier

The entry trading product may be free or low-cost, but must be limited.

Possible limits:

- maximum capital: around $100 or governance-defined equivalent;
- limited strategies;
- restricted leverage;
- limited duration;
- required certification;
- NFT-based access;
- risk alerts;
- ACS-guided configuration.

---

## 7. Strategy Documentation

Before public release, document:

- strategy objective;
- market conditions;
- timeframe;
- capital used;
- risk rules;
- drawdown;
- performance;
- failure modes;
- exchange compatibility;
- operational incidents.

---

## 8. Marketing Rule

Marketing must be based on documented operation, not promise.

Do not market guaranteed profit.

Market:

- discipline;
- automation;
- risk control;
- transparency;
- education;
- ecosystem integration;
- documented progress.

---

## 9. ACS Integration

ACS agents should assist with:

- configuration guidance;
- parameter recommendation;
- risk monitoring;
- strategy selection;
- market condition interpretation;
- user warnings;
- reporting.

---

## 10. Final Principle

Trading Core should prove Axodus can generate operational value before the ecosystem asks the public for trust.
