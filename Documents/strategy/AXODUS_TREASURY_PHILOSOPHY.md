# AXODUS TREASURY PHILOSOPHY

Version: 0.1 Draft  
Status: Strategic Doctrine

---

## 1. Core Statement

Treasury is sacred.

The Axodus treasury model must prioritize preservation, productivity, transparency and sustainable distribution.

Treasury should never be treated as idle capital, marketing fuel or a short-term spending reserve.

---

## 2. Treasury Purpose

Treasury exists to:

- fund ecosystem development;
- activate products;
- preserve operational runway;
- generate sustainable revenue;
- support partner DAOs;
- create long-term holder value;
- distribute earnings when economically justified.

---

## 3. Treasury Layers

### Root Treasury
The strategic treasury of the Axodus ecosystem.

### Partner DAO Treasury
Independent treasury controlled by each DAO.

### Product Treasury / Vault
Capital allocated to a specific product, strategy or protocol.

### Reserve Treasury
Capital protected for safety, emergencies and long-term resilience.

---

## 4. Capital Principles

### 4.1 Preservation First
No strategy is valid if it threatens systemic survival.

### 4.2 Productive Allocation
Capital should be allocated toward activities that generate measurable value.

### 4.3 Transparency
Treasury allocation, movement and performance must be auditable.

### 4.4 Governance Execution
Capital-sensitive operations must be governance-defined and proposal-driven.

### 4.5 No Artificial Yield
Yield must come from real economic activity, not unsustainable emissions.

---

## 5. Distribution Philosophy

Distribution should happen only when revenue is real, measured and sustainable.

Possible distribution paths:

- holder rewards;
- DAO member shares;
- treasury reinvestment;
- product development;
- reserve growth;
- buyback/burn mechanisms when approved;
- grants or incentives.

---

## 6. Risk Tiers

### Tier 0 — Reserve
Low-risk capital preservation.

### Tier 1 — Conservative Yield
Stable, low-risk strategies.

### Tier 2 — Productive Growth
Mining, staking, lending, LP or revenue-generating products.

### Tier 3 — Active Strategy
Trading, arbitrage, tactical allocations and agent-supported execution.

### Tier 4 — Experimental
New strategies, integrations or products under strict limits.

---

## 7. Reporting Requirements

Every treasury allocation should include:

- objective;
- risk tier;
- capital amount;
- expected role;
- performance metrics;
- loss limits;
- responsible operator/DAO;
- reporting cadence.

---

## 8. Final Principle

Axodus should grow because its treasury becomes more productive, not because its token becomes more speculative.
