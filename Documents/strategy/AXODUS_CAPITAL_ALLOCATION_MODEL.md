# AXODUS CAPITAL ALLOCATION MODEL

Version: 0.1 Draft  
Status: Economic Operations Model

---

## 1. Objective

Define how Axodus and Partner DAOs allocate capital across products, strategies and operational needs through governance.

---

## 2. Allocation Philosophy

Capital allocation must be:

- intentional;
- measurable;
- governed;
- risk-adjusted;
- transparent;
- reversible when needed.

---

## 3. Allocation Categories

### Reserve
Capital preserved for safety and operational continuity.

### Product Activation
Capital used to activate ecosystem products such as Mining, Trading, DeFi, Compute or BBA.

### Growth
Capital used to expand user acquisition, product development and strategic partnerships.

### Yield
Capital deployed into revenue-generating strategies.

### Experimentation
Small, capped allocations for testing new strategies or products.

---

## 4. Product Allocation Examples

### Trading Core
Initial capital ignition and strategy validation.

### Mining
Hashrate acquisition and yield operations.

### DeFi / Staking
External protocol yield and treasury diversification.

### Marketplace / Academy
Revenue infrastructure and user acquisition.

### Compute
GPU and AI infrastructure expansion.

### BBA
Revenue-generating service operations.

---

## 5. Decision Flow

```txt
Capital Need
    ↓
Proposal
    ↓
Risk Classification
    ↓
Governance Vote
    ↓
Execution
    ↓
Monitoring
    ↓
Reporting
    ↓
Reallocation / Expansion / Exit
```

---

## 6. Minimum Proposal Requirements

Every allocation proposal should include:

- purpose;
- amount;
- product/strategy;
- risk classification;
- expected outcome;
- maximum drawdown or loss tolerance;
- reporting cadence;
- exit criteria;
- responsible DAO/operator/agent.

---

## 7. Scaling Rule

No allocation should scale before:

- it has been tested;
- risks are known;
- performance is documented;
- operational process is stable;
- governance accepts expansion.

---

## 8. Final Principle

Axodus should allocate capital like a disciplined operating company, not like a speculative protocol.
