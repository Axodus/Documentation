# AXODUS ECONOMIC MODEL

Version: 0.1 Draft  
Status: Economic Framework

---

## 1. Overview

The Axodus economic model coordinates autonomous DAOs, treasury operations, product ecosystems, agents and decentralized infrastructure through governance and aligned incentives.

The model prioritizes real revenue, operational efficiency and sustainable capital distribution over inflationary growth.

---

## 2. Core Components

- Governance
- Root Treasury
- Partner DAO Treasuries
- Products
- Marketplace
- Academy
- $Neurons
- Autonomous Agents
- Compute Network
- Business / BBA

---

## 3. Root Economy

The Root DAO coordinates:

- ecosystem treasury policies;
- constitutional governance;
- strategic allocation;
- ecosystem incentives;
- product activation;
- risk controls.

---

## 4. DAO Economies

Each DAO may operate:

- independent treasury;
- investment strategies;
- member rewards;
- client onboarding;
- product participation;
- revenue distribution.

---

## 5. Product Economy

### Mining
Hashrate acquisition, miner management and mining yield.

### Trading
Internal capital ignition, trading products, strategy validation and API-based user trading access.

### DeFi / Staking
Yield generation through external DeFi ecosystems and governance-approved strategies.

### Marketplace
Distribution of products, services, NFTs, licenses, courses, agents and infrastructure.

### Academy
Knowledge economy, Proof of Knowledge and Learning to Win.

### MCP Servers
Infrastructure subscriptions and agent tooling.

### Compute Network
GPU and inference economy.

### BBA
AI-powered business, marketing and growth services for blockchain/tokenized projects.

$Neurons may be used for:

## 9. Treasury Philosophy

Treasury is not a spending account. Treasury is the productive capital layer of the ecosystem.

The objective is:

- preserve capital;
- produce revenue;
- reinvest intelligently;
- distribute sustainably;
- fund ecosystem growth.

---

## 10. APR Philosophy

APR should be a consequence of operational efficiency, not token inflation.

Axodus must avoid artificial yield promises.

---

## 11. Risk Management

The ecosystem may implement:

- treasury diversification;
- governance safeguards;
- allocation limits;
- strategy limits;
- emergency controls;
- audit requirements;
- risk committees;
- agent-based monitoring.

---

## 12. Final Vision

Axodus aims to establish a governed economic infrastructure where autonomous organizations coordinate capital, services, intelligence and infrastructure through decentralized governance.
