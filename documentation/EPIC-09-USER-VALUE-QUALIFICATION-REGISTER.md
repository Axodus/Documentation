- Business is operational infrastructure, not a software house, freelancer marketplace, or autonomous authority.
- Business runtime, Governance, Financial, and ACS bridges remain mock/read-only or architectural preparation.
- Trinity and ACS are advisory unless separate authority, implementation, and activation evidence exists.
- Marketplace listings, payments, settlement, certification effects, rewards, and commercial outcomes remain conditional.
- BBA cannot promise performance, adoption, revenue, legal compliance, or verified partnerships.
- Missing implementation and activation evidence routes to `DEFER_TO_EPIC_10`.
